§ Who This Is For § 适合人群

Who Needs Complex Care Coordination

哪些患者需要复杂专科协调

Multi-Specialty Conditions

涉及多专科的复杂病情

Your condition involves two or more medical specialties and requires coordinated evaluation by specialists from different departments, possibly at different hospitals.

您的病情涉及两个及以上医学专科，需要不同科室（可能跨医院）的专家协同评估。

Rare Disease or Diagnostic Mystery

罕见病或疑难杂症

Your condition is rare, atypical, or has resisted clear diagnosis despite multiple evaluations. You need access to specialists with deep experience in uncommon conditions.

您的病情罕见、不典型，或经多次评估仍未能明确诊断。您需要具有罕见病丰富经验的专家。

MDT Consultation Required

需要多学科团队会诊

Your case would benefit from a formal multidisciplinary team consultation where specialists from several departments discuss your case together and propose a unified treatment plan.

您的病情适合正式的多学科团队会诊（MDT），由多个科室的专家共同讨论并制定统一的治疗方案。

Extended Treatment in Beijing

需要长时间在京治疗

Your treatment plan requires an extended stay in Beijing (more than 2 weeks), including surgery, hospitalization, or a series of procedures that need ongoing coordination.

您的治疗方案需要在北京停留较长时间（超过两周），包括手术、住院或一系列需要持续协调的检查与治疗。

Surgical Planning & Perioperative Support

手术安排与围手术期协调

You are planning to undergo surgery in Beijing and need coordination covering pre-operative preparation, the procedure itself, and post-operative follow-up care.

您计划在北京接受手术，需要涵盖术前准备、手术过程及术后随访的全程协调。

§ How It Works § 服务流程

How Complex Care Coordination Works

复杂专科协调如何运作

Complex care coordination goes significantly deeper than a standard specialist consultation. The process is more thorough, spans more institutions, and typically covers a longer time frame.

复杂专科协调远比普通专科会诊更深入。流程更全面、涉及更多机构、覆盖更长时间跨度。

01

In-Depth Case Assessment

深度病情评估

You submit your complete medical records (imaging, lab results, operative reports, pathology, etc.). Our coordination team conducts a thorough review and prepares a detailed bilingual case summary.

您提交完整病历资料（影像、检验、手术记录、病理等）。我们的协调团队进行全面审阅，准备详尽的双语病例摘要。

02

Multi-Specialist Matching

多学科专家匹配

Based on the case assessment, we identify specialists across multiple departments — potentially spanning 2–3 hospitals — whose combined expertise addresses all aspects of your condition.

根据病例评估，我们在多个科室中匹配专家——可能跨 2–3 家医院——使其综合专长覆盖您病情的各个方面。

03

MDT Consultation Arrangement

MDT 会诊安排

Where appropriate, we coordinate a formal multidisciplinary team (MDT) consultation, bringing together specialists from relevant departments to discuss your case collectively.

在适当情况下，我们协调正式的多学科团队会诊（MDT），组织相关科室专家共同讨论您的病情。

04

Inpatient / Surgical Coordination

住院/手术协调

If your treatment plan requires hospitalization or surgery, we coordinate bed scheduling, pre-operative preparation, and ensure all logistical elements are aligned.

如需住院或手术，我们协调床位安排、术前准备，确保所有后勤环节到位。

05

Continuous On-the-Ground Support

在京期间持续协调支持

Throughout your stay in Beijing, a dedicated coordinator remains available for scheduling adjustments, interpretation needs, hospital liaison, and any emerging requirements.

在您留京期间，专属协调员随时待命，处理排期调整、翻译需求、医院沟通及任何新增需求。

06

Post-Treatment Rehabilitation Linkage

术后/诊后康复衔接

After treatment, we help coordinate follow-up appointments, rehabilitation referrals, and translate discharge summaries so you can continue care smoothly after returning home.

治疗结束后，我们协助安排复诊、康复转介，翻译出院摘要，确保您回国后能顺利继续治疗。

§ Service Boundary § 服务边界

Important: Understanding Our Role

重要说明：了解我们的角色

YuanU is a medical-coordination service company, not a medical institution.

远游是医疗协调服务公司，非医疗机构。

  • All diagnoses, examinations, treatments, and surgical procedures are provided independently by licensed physicians at cooperating hospitals.
  • YuanU does not provide medical opinions, make clinical recommendations, or guarantee specific clinical outcomes.
  • YuanU does not speak for or represent any hospital. We coordinate logistics, translation, and scheduling on behalf of the patient.
  • MDT consultation outcomes depend on the participating physicians' clinical judgment; YuanU facilitates the process but does not influence medical decisions.
  • Scheduling for specialists, operating rooms, and hospital beds is subject to hospital availability; we coordinate based on what is available but cannot guarantee specific timelines.
  • 所有诊断、检查、治疗和手术均由合作医院的执业医师独立提供。
  • 远游不出具医学意见、不做临床推荐、不就特定临床结果做承诺。
  • 远游不代表任何医院发言。我们为患者协调后勤、翻译和排期。
  • MDT 会诊结果取决于参与医师的临床判断；远游促进流程但不影响医疗决策。
  • 专家、手术室和床位的排期以医院可用性为准；我们根据实际可用情况协调，但无法保证特定时间线。
