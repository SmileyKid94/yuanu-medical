Other Services — non-medical support around your hospital schedule

其它服务 — 围绕您的就医行程的非医疗支持

These are non-medical services YuanU offers on request around your hospital schedule. They are not YuanU's core business. They exist because many international patients coming to Beijing for medical care want basic logistical and local support in the days between appointments and reports.

以下为远游可在您的就医行程之上按需提供的非医疗服务。这些不是远游的核心业务；之所以保留，是因为多数来京就医的国际患者希望在等待报告 / 检查间隔的时间里有合理的本地支持。所有项目均围绕您的医院日程安排，非主推产品。

These items will not appear in the main navigation. They sit at the bottom of YuanU's service stack and are billed independently from medical coordination. 这些条目不会出现在主导航中；它们位于远游服务栈的底层，与医疗协调服务分开计费。

Boundary & billing · §8.3 边界与计费 · §8.3

Four notes on these services

关于其它服务的几点说明

  1. Non-medical, available on request only. These services have nothing to do with clinical diagnosis or treatment. YuanU does not bundle them by default and does not tie them to medical coordination. 非医疗、按需附加。这些服务与医疗诊疗无关；远游不强制打包、不与医疗协调服务捆绑销售。
  2. Billed independently. Each item is quoted separately by hour or per occurrence, and appears as a distinct invoice line item — not folded into the medical coordination fee. 独立计费。每项按小时 / 次单独报价，独立发票条目；不并入医疗协调服务费。
  3. Backed by a professional travel-service team. YuanU has a professional travel-service team with proven experience, capable of providing full itinerary planning, tour-guide service, hotel booking and concierge support. 由专业服务团队承接。远游具有专业的服务团队及经验，可以提供完整的旅游行程规划 / 导游服务 / 酒店预定及礼宾服务。
  4. Why we keep these items at all. Most international patients coming to Beijing for care have told us they wanted basic local support in the days between examination and report. That feedback is the only reason these items exist — not business expansion. 保留的原因。多数来京就医的国际患者反馈，希望在等待报告 / 检查间隔的几天里有基础的本地支持。这是远游保留这些服务的唯一原因 — 不是为了拓展业务。

Service Boundary. Other services are non-medical support items, offered on request. YuanU's core business is inbound medical coordination; non-medical travel and concierge services are provided by YuanU's own travel-service team under separate billing. See the full Service Boundary statement on the About page.

服务边界。其它服务为非医疗类支持服务，按需提供。远游核心业务是入境医疗协调；非医疗类的旅游与礼宾服务由远游自有的旅行服务团队承接，独立计费。完整服务边界见 About 页。
