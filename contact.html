Privacy Notice · §9.3 隐私声明 · §9.3

How we handle the information you submit

关于您提交信息的处理

YuanU follows the requirements of the Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China (PIPL) and applicable medical-information protection rules. All information you provide through this form or other channels is handled as follows.

远游遵循中国《个人信息保护法》（PIPL）与医疗信息保护的相关要求。您通过本表单或其他渠道提供的所有信息，按以下方式处理。

  1. Scope of use. Used only for internal coordination of this inquiry, for appointment alignment with BFH·IMD and partner hospitals, and for issuing the necessary appointment confirmation letter for visa support. 使用范围。仅用于本次咨询的内部协调、与 BFH·IMD 等合作医院的预约对接、必要的签证支持函出具。
  2. No sharing with third parties. YuanU will not share your personal or medical information with any third party unrelated to this inquiry (including but not limited to insurers, marketing channels, or other medical institutions). 不向第三方分享。远游不会向任何与本次咨询无关的第三方（包括但不限于保险公司、市场推广方、其他医疗机构）分享您的个人或医疗信息。
  3. Encrypted transfer. Medical materials are received via encrypted email or secure file transfer links. 加密传输。医疗资料通过加密邮件 / 安全文件传输链接接收。
  4. Retention period. Basic contact information is retained for 2 years for customer records; medical records are retained according to hospital policy; YuanU's internal working translations are destroyed within 30 days after delivery. 保留期限。基础联系信息保留 2 年用于客户档案；医疗资料按 BFH 医院规定保留；远游内部翻译工作副本在交付后 30 天内销毁。
  5. Your rights. You may, at any time, email us to: (a) request a copy of your information; (b) request corrections; (c) request deletion within reasonable limits (hospital-side reports are retained by the hospital under its own rules, and are not within YuanU's deletable scope). 您的权利。您可以随时通过邮件要求远游：①查阅 / 复制您的信息 ②更正 ③在合理范围内删除（医院端报告由医院按规定保留，不在远游可删除范围内）。
  6. Cross-border transfer. If you are a patient based outside mainland China, your information may be stored and processed within mainland China — this is required for the coordination work you have asked us to perform on your behalf. 跨境传输。如您是中国大陆境外的患者，您的信息可能在中国境内存储和处理；这是基于您选择来中国就医的协调需要。

Note on PIPL vs HIPAA. China's PIPL and the United States' HIPAA are not equivalent frameworks. We follow PIPL as the governing law for handling your information; if your insurer or home-country physician requires HIPAA-style documentation, please tell your coordinator at the inquiry stage.

关于 PIPL 与 HIPAA 的说明。中国《个人信息保护法》（PIPL）与美国 HIPAA 并非等价框架；远游按 PIPL 处理您的信息。如您本国保险公司或医师要求 HIPAA 形式的合规材料，请在咨询阶段告知您的协调员。
