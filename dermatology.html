远游为在京国际患者协调北京三甲医院皮肤科就医服务。从湿疹、银屑病与痤疮管理，到过敏原检查、皮肤肿物评估、激光治疗咨询与脱发诊治，我们提供预约挂号、检查协调、双语陪诊与报告翻译——让您清晰、从容地对接北京皮肤科医疗资源。

§ 1 · Common Conditions § 1 · 常见病种

1. Common dermatology visit scenarios

1. 皮肤科常见就诊场景

YuanU coordinates care across a range of common dermatology conditions. The following list describes typical situations — it is not exhaustive and does not constitute medical advice.

远游协调覆盖多种常见皮肤科病种的就诊场景。以下列举典型情况，非完整清单，亦不构成医学建议。

  • Eczema / dermatitis: coordinating consultations for atopic dermatitis, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, and other inflammatory skin conditions.
  • 湿疹 / 皮炎：协调特应性皮炎、接触性皮炎、脂溢性皮炎等炎症性皮肤病的专科就诊。
  • Psoriasis: coordinating visits for assessment, severity evaluation, and discussion of available management options.
  • 银屑病：协调银屑病评估、严重程度判断及治疗方案讨论。
  • Acne: coordinating consultations for moderate to severe acne, including discussion of treatment approaches with a specialist.
  • 痤疮：协调中重度痤疮的专科就诊，含治疗方案讨论。
  • Allergic skin conditions: coordinating visits for urticaria, drug eruptions, and other allergic dermatoses requiring specialist assessment.
  • 过敏性皮肤病：协调荨麻疹、药疹等过敏性皮肤病的专科评估。
  • Skin masses / growths: coordinating evaluations for moles, cysts, lipomas, and other skin or subcutaneous lesions.
  • 皮肤肿物：协调痣、囊肿、脂肪瘤等皮肤或皮下肿物的专科评估。
  • Laser treatment consultation: coordinating visits for evaluation and discussion of laser-based interventions (e.g., pigmentation, vascular lesions).
  • 激光治疗咨询：协调激光干预（如色素性、血管性病变）相关的评估与方案讨论。
  • Hair loss (alopecia): coordinating visits for hair loss assessment, pattern evaluation, and management options.
  • 脱发：协调脱发评估、脱发模式判断及管理方案讨论。

Important note

重要提示

YuanU is a coordination service. We do not provide medical opinions, recommend specific examinations, or suggest clinical pathways. All decisions about what to examine, how often, and which treatment options to pursue are made by the licensed attending physician at the partner hospital.

远游是协调服务机构。我们不提供医学意见、不建议具体检查项目、不规划临床路径。所有关于检查内容、频率和治疗方案的决策均由合作医院执业医师做出。

§ 2 · Why Beijing § 2 · 为何选择北京

2. Why Beijing for dermatology care

2. 北京皮肤科就医优势

Beijing's tertiary hospitals host some of China's leading dermatology departments, with broad outpatient volume and advanced diagnostic capabilities. Several key advantages for international patients include:

北京三甲医院拥有全国领先的皮肤科，门诊量大且诊断能力先进。对于国际患者，主要优势包括：

  • PKU First Hospital Dermatology (北大第一医院皮肤科): consistently ranked among the top dermatology departments nationally, known for clinical research in autoimmune skin diseases, genetic dermatoses, and patch testing.
  • 北京大学第一医院皮肤科：连续位列全国皮肤科前列，在自身免疫性皮肤病、遗传性皮肤病和斑贴试验等临床研究方面享有盛誉。
  • PUMCH (Peking Union Medical College Hospital): its dermatology department is widely recognized for complex and rare skin conditions, with strong interdisciplinary diagnostic support.
  • PUMCH（北京协和医院）：其皮肤科在疑难罕见皮肤病方面全国知名，具备强大的跨学科诊断支持。
  • BFH (Beijing Friendship Hospital): offers comprehensive outpatient dermatology services including laser and cosmetic dermatology.
  • BFH（北京友谊医院）：提供综合性皮肤门诊服务，含激光治疗与美容皮肤科。
  • High outpatient volume: leading Beijing dermatology departments see tens of thousands of outpatient visits annually, giving physicians broad exposure to diverse presentations.
  • 门诊量大：北京头部皮肤科年门诊量数以万计，医师接触的病种分布广泛。

Hospitals we coordinate with

我们协调合作的医院

YuanU coordinates care at multiple tertiary hospitals in Beijing, including PKU First Hospital, PUMCH, and BFH-IMD (Beijing Friendship Hospital International Medical Department), depending on the patient's needs and physician availability.

远游在北京多家三甲医院提供协调服务，包括北京大学第一医院、PUMCH（北京协和医院）、BFH-IMD（北京友谊医院国际医疗部）等，具体视患者需求与医师排班而定。

Hospital choice is based on clinical fit and availability, not on any commercial arrangement. YuanU does not receive referral fees from any hospital.

医院选择基于临床匹配度与可预约性，不涉及任何商业安排。远游不收取任何医院的转介费用。

§ 6 · Service Boundary § 6 · 服务边界

6. Service Boundary & disclaimer

6. 服务边界与免责

YuanU is a coordination service, not a medical institution

远游是协调服务机构，非医疗机构

YuanU provides administrative coordination and bilingual interpretation for dermatology visits at partner tertiary hospitals. We do not provide medical diagnoses, do not recommend specific examinations or treatment plans, and do not interpret clinical results. All medical decisions are made independently by licensed physicians at the partner hospitals.

远游为合作三甲医院的皮肤科就诊提供行政协调与双语翻译服务。我们不提供医学诊断、不建议具体检查或治疗方案、不解读临床结果。所有医学决策均由合作医院的执业医师独立做出。

Specific exclusions

明确排除项

  • YuanU does not perform any clinical examination or procedure.
  • 远游不执行任何临床检查或操作。
  • YuanU coordinators do not interpret allergy test results, dermoscopic findings, biopsy results, or any other diagnostic data.
  • 远游协调员不解读过敏检测结果、皮肤镜发现、活检结果或任何其他诊断数据。
  • YuanU does not recommend or endorse any specific medication, topical product, treatment procedure, or clinical pathway.
  • 远游不建议或推荐任何特定药物、外用产品、操作或临床路径。
  • YuanU does not guarantee any clinical outcome or skin improvement.
  • 远游不就任何临床结果或皮肤改善做承诺。
