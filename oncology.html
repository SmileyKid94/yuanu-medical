Oncology Consultation in Beijing — Coordinated by YuanU

肿瘤科就医协调 — 远游医疗

YuanU coordinates oncology consultations at leading tertiary hospitals in Beijing for international patients seeking second opinions, multi-disciplinary team (MDT) consultations, or follow-up care. We arrange specialist appointments, translate medical records and imaging, provide bilingual on-site support, and help you navigate the healthcare system with clarity and confidence — all as independent administrative coordination only.

远游为在京国际患者协调北京三甲医院肿瘤科就医服务，包括二次诊疗、多学科会诊（MDT）及随访复查。我们提供专科预约、病历与影像翻译、双语现场陪同，帮助您清晰、从容地对接医疗资源——仅限独立行政协调服务。

§ 1 · Common Scenarios § 1 · 常见场景

1. Common oncology consultation scenarios

1. 肿瘤科常见咨询场景

YuanU coordinates care across a range of common oncology visit scenarios. The following list describes typical situations — it is not exhaustive and does not constitute medical advice.

远游协调覆盖多种常见肿瘤科就诊场景。以下列举典型情况，非完整清单，亦不构成医学建议。

  • Second opinion — coordinating consultations with specialists at Beijing tertiary hospitals to obtain an independent review of a diagnosis or proposed treatment approach.
  • 二次诊疗（Second Opinion） — 协调北京三甲医院专科医师就诊，以获得对诊断或拟议治疗方案的独立复核意见。
  • Multi-disciplinary team (MDT) consultation — coordinating arrangements for MDT discussions involving oncologists, surgeons, radiologists, and pathologists.
  • 多学科会诊（MDT） — 协调涉及肿瘤内科、外科、影像科、病理科等多学科团队讨论安排。
  • Chemotherapy consultation — coordinating specialist visits to discuss chemotherapy regimens, side-effect management, or treatment sequencing.
  • 化疗咨询 — 协调专科就诊，讨论化疗方案、副作用管理或治疗顺序。
  • Targeted therapy assessment — coordinating evaluations related to targeted therapy or immunotherapy options, including genetic testing coordination.
  • 靶向评估 — 协调靶向治疗或免疫治疗相关评估，含基因检测协调。
  • Follow-up & surveillance — coordinating post-treatment follow-up visits, imaging surveillance, and tumor marker monitoring.
  • 随访复查 — 协调治疗后随访就诊、影像监测及肿瘤标志物检查。

Important note

重要提示

YuanU is a coordination service. We do not provide medical opinions, recommend specific examinations, suggest treatment plans, or offer prognosis. All clinical decisions are made by the licensed attending physician at the partner hospital.

远游是协调服务机构。我们不提供医学意见、不建议具体检查或治疗方案、不提供预后判断。所有临床决策均由合作医院执业医师做出。

§ 2 · Why Beijing § 2 · 为何选择北京

2. Why Beijing for oncology care

2. 北京肿瘤科就医优势

Beijing is home to China's leading oncology institutions, offering high patient volumes, broad subspecialty coverage, and access to advanced diagnostic and therapeutic technologies. Key advantages include:

北京拥有全国领先的肿瘤医疗机构，具备高患者量、广泛的亚专科覆盖以及先进的诊断与治疗技术。主要优势包括：

  • Cancer Hospital, Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences (CAMS) — China's national cancer center, widely recognized as one of the leading comprehensive oncology hospitals in the country, with extensive subspecialty departments.
  • 中国医学科学院肿瘤医院 — 国家癌症中心，全国公认的领先综合性肿瘤医院之一，亚专科科室齐全。
  • PUMCH (Peking Union Medical College Hospital) — Oncology Department — its medical oncology department is among the most respected in the country, with strong capabilities in complex malignancies and rare tumors.
  • PUMCH（北京协和医院）肿瘤内科 — 其肿瘤内科是全国最受尊重的学科之一，在复杂恶性肿瘤与罕见肿瘤方面实力突出。
  • Peking University Cancer Hospital — a major oncology center affiliated with Peking University, known for comprehensive cancer care and clinical research.
  • 北京大学肿瘤医院 — 北京大学附属大型肿瘤中心，以综合肿瘤诊疗与临床研究见长。
  • BFH (Beijing Friendship Hospital) — Oncology Department — provides comprehensive oncology outpatient and inpatient services.
  • BFH（北京友谊医院）肿瘤科 — 提供全面的肿瘤门诊与住院服务。

Hospitals we coordinate with

我们协调合作的医院

YuanU coordinates care at multiple tertiary hospitals in Beijing, including the Cancer Hospital CAMS, PUMCH, Peking University Cancer Hospital, BFH, and others depending on the patient's needs and physician availability.

远游在北京多家三甲医院提供协调服务，包括中国医学科学院肿瘤医院、PUMCH（北京协和医院）、北京大学肿瘤医院、BFH（北京友谊医院）等，具体视患者需求与医师排班而定。

Hospital choice is based on clinical fit and availability, not on any commercial arrangement. YuanU does not receive referral fees from any hospital.

医院选择基于临床匹配度与可预约性，不涉及任何商业安排。远游不收取任何医院的转介费用。

§ 6 · Important § 6 · 重要声明

6. What we do not do

6. 我们不做的事

⚠ YuanU does not make prognosis judgments or recommend treatment plans

⚠ 远游不做预后判断、不建议治疗方案

YuanU is an administrative coordination service. We do not make prognosis judgments, do not recommend or evaluate treatment plans, and do not interpret pathology results, imaging findings, or tumor markers. All clinical opinions come exclusively from licensed physicians at the partner hospitals. If you need a medical opinion, your consultation with the hospital physician is the appropriate channel.

远游是行政协调服务机构。我们不做预后判断、不建议或评估治疗方案、不解读病理结果、影像学发现或肿瘤标志物。所有临床意见均来自合作医院的执业医师。如需医学意见，与医院医师的会诊是适当的渠道。
