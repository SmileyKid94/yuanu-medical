Pediatric Care Coordination in Beijing — Coordinated by YuanU

儿童健康就医协调 — 远游医疗协调

YuanU provides pediatric care coordination at leading tertiary hospitals in Beijing for expat and international families. From developmental assessments and common childhood infections to allergy and asthma management, routine well-child visits, and vaccine consultations, we arrange appointments, provide bilingual parent-accompanied interpretation, translate medical records, and help your family navigate Beijing's pediatric healthcare system with confidence.

远游为在京外籍家庭和国际家庭提供北京三甲医院的儿童就医协调服务。从发育评估、常见儿童感染到过敏与哮喘管理、常规体检及疫苗咨询，我们提供预约挂号、家长陪同双语口译、病历翻译，帮助您的家庭从容对接北京的儿科医疗资源。

§ 1 · Common Scenarios § 1 · 常见就诊场景

1. Common pediatric visit scenarios

1. 儿童常见就诊场景

YuanU coordinates care across a range of common pediatric visit scenarios for expat and international families in Beijing. The following list describes typical situations — it is not exhaustive and does not constitute medical advice.

远游为在京外籍和国际家庭协调多种常见儿科就诊场景。以下列举典型情况，非完整清单，亦不构成医学建议。

  • Developmental assessment — coordination of visits for growth monitoring, developmental milestone evaluation, and early childhood development concerns.
  • 发育评估 — 协调生长发育监测、发育里程碑评估及幼儿发育相关就诊。
  • Common infections — coordinating visits for upper respiratory infections, gastrointestinal illnesses, and other common childhood infections.
  • 常见感染 — 协调上呼吸道感染、胃肠道疾病及其他儿童常见感染相关就诊。
  • Allergy & asthma management — coordination of specialist consultations for allergic rhinitis, food allergies, eczema, and pediatric asthma follow-up.
  • 过敏与哮喘管理 — 协调过敏性鼻炎、食物过敏、湿疹及儿童哮喘随访的专科就诊。
  • Well-child visits — arranging routine pediatric check-ups including growth assessment, nutritional guidance, and age-appropriate screening.
  • 儿童体检 — 安排常规儿科检查，含生长评估、营养指导及适龄筛查。
  • Vaccine consultations — coordinating consultations regarding vaccination schedules, catch-up immunizations, and cross-country vaccine record alignment.
  • 疫苗咨询 — 协调疫苗接种计划、补种安排及跨国疫苗记录对接相关咨询。

Important note

重要提示

YuanU is a coordination service. We do not provide medical opinions, recommend specific examinations, or suggest clinical pathways. All decisions about your child's care are made by the licensed attending physician at the partner hospital. We do not provide diagnostic or prescriptive services of any kind.

远游是协调服务机构。我们不提供医学意见、不建议具体检查项目、不规划临床路径。所有关于您孩子就医的决策均由合作医院执业医师做出。我们不提供任何形式的诊断或处方服务。

§ 2 · Why Beijing § 2 · 为何选择北京

2. Why Beijing for pediatric care

2. 北京儿科就医优势

Beijing is home to some of China's most prominent pediatric hospitals and departments. For international families, key advantages include:

北京拥有中国最知名的儿科医院和科室。对于国际家庭而言，主要优势包括：

  • Beijing Children's Hospital (BCH): one of the largest pediatric hospitals in China, covering virtually all pediatric subspecialties with enormous outpatient volume and clinical breadth.
  • 北京儿童医院（BCH）：中国规模最大的儿科医院之一，几乎覆盖所有儿科亚专科，门诊量巨大、临床覆盖面广。
  • Capital Institute of Pediatrics (SIH): a leading pediatric research and clinical institution affiliated with Capital Medical University, known for its depth in pediatric subspecialties.
  • 首都儿科研究所（SIH）：首都医科大学附属的领先儿科研究与临床机构，以儿科亚专科的深度见长。
  • PUMCH Pediatrics (Peking Union Medical College Hospital): the pediatric department of China's premier academic medical center, offering comprehensive pediatric subspecialty care.
  • PUMCH 儿科（北京协和医院）：中国顶尖学术医学中心的儿科科室，提供全面的儿科亚专科服务。
  • BFH Pediatrics (Beijing Friendship Hospital): the pediatric department is recognized for its clinical capabilities and integrated care approach.
  • BFH 儿科（北京友谊医院）：儿科以临床能力和综合诊疗模式著称。

Hospitals we coordinate with

我们协调合作的医院

YuanU coordinates pediatric care at multiple tertiary hospitals in Beijing, including BCH, SIH, PUMCH, and BFH, depending on the child's needs, subspecialty requirements, and physician availability.

远游在北京多家三甲医院提供儿科协调服务，包括北京儿童医院（BCH）、首都儿科研究所（SIH）、PUMCH 及 BFH，具体视患儿需求、亚专科需求与医师排班而定。

Hospital choice is based on clinical fit and availability, not on any commercial arrangement. YuanU does not receive referral fees from any hospital.

医院选择基于临床匹配度与可预约性，不涉及任何商业安排。远游不收取任何医院的转介费用。

§ 6 · Notes for Families § 6 · 家庭须知

6. Practical notes for families

6. 家庭实用须知

The following practical notes may help expat and international families prepare for pediatric visits in Beijing. These are coordination-related observations, not medical advice.

以下实用须知可帮助外籍和国际家庭为在北京的儿科就诊做好准备。这些是协调方面的观察，非医学建议。

Vaccine record bridging

疫苗记录衔接

China uses its own National Immunization Program (NIP) schedule, which may differ from your home country's schedule. When visiting Beijing, bring your child's original vaccination records. YuanU can help organize and translate these records for the physician's reference, facilitating a discussion about schedule alignment or catch-up plans. We do not make recommendations about which vaccines to administer.

中国使用国家免疫规划（NIP）接种程序，可能与您的本国程序不同。来京就诊时请携带孩子的原始疫苗接种记录。远游可帮助整理并翻译这些记录供医师参考，协助对接计划或补种方案的讨论。我们不就接种何种疫苗提出建议。

School examination requirements

学校体检要求

International schools in Beijing may require annual health checks or specific forms completed by a physician. YuanU can coordinate the appointment and assist with form translation, but the physician at the hospital completes all medical sections independently.

北京的国际学校可能要求年度健康检查或由医师填写特定表格。远游可协调预约并协助表格翻译，但医院医师独立完成所有医学部分的填写。

Emergency situations

紧急情况处理

For pediatric emergencies, please call 120 (China's emergency number) or go directly to the nearest hospital emergency department. YuanU's coordination service is not an emergency service and cannot guarantee same-day or immediate appointments. In urgent situations, prioritize getting your child to emergency care first.

遇到儿童紧急情况，请拨打 120（中国急救电话）或直接前往最近的医院急诊科。远游的协调服务不是急救服务，无法保证当日或即时预约。紧急情况下请优先确保孩子获得急诊救治。

Reminder

提醒

YuanU coordinators are not medically trained and do not provide clinical triage. If you are unsure whether your child's condition is urgent, err on the side of caution and seek emergency care immediately.

远游协调员不具备医学资质，不提供临床分诊。如果您不确定孩子的病情是否紧急，请宁可谨慎处理，立即寻求急诊救治。

7. Service Boundary & disclaimer

7. 服务边界与免责

  • 远游不建议或推荐任何特定药物、疫苗品牌、剂量或儿童临床路径。
