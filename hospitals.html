Beijing Friendship Hospital (BFH) · International Medical Department

北京友谊医院 · 国际医疗部

Beijing Friendship Hospital is a Grade-A tertiary comprehensive hospital and the host institution of the National Clinical Research Center for Digestive Diseases. It is one of Beijing's most established hospitals for international patient services, with a dedicated International Medical Department (IMD) that provides a full range of outpatient, diagnostic, and inpatient services.

北京友谊医院是三级甲等综合医院，国家消化系统疾病临床医学研究中心挂靠单位。它是北京最早开展国际医疗服务的医院之一，设有独立的国际医疗部（IMD），提供完整的门诊、检查和住院服务。

Key Strengths

优势特色

  • Gastroenterology & Digestive Surgery — National-level center of excellence; leading endoscopy and GI surgery capabilities.
  • 消化内科与消化外科 — 国家级重点学科；领先的内镜与胃肠外科能力。
  • Cardiology — Strong interventional cardiology and cardiac imaging programs.
  • 心血管内科 — 强大的介入心脏病学和心脏影像项目。
  • Endocrinology — Comprehensive diabetes and metabolic disease management.
  • 内分泌科 — 全面的糖尿病与代谢病管理。

IMD Features

国际医疗部特色

  • Dedicated outpatient area with separate waiting and consultation rooms
  • 独立诊区，设有独立候诊和诊室
  • Multilingual reception support
  • 多语言接待服务
  • International commercial insurance direct billing capability
  • 支持国际商业保险直付

Peking Union Medical College Hospital (PUMCH)

北京协和医院

Peking Union Medical College Hospital is widely regarded as one of China's foremost comprehensive hospitals and has consistently ranked at or near the top of national hospital rankings. Affiliated with the Peking Union Medical College (China's premier medical school), PUMCH is known for its rigorous academic standards and its strength in handling difficult, rare, and complex cases.

北京协和医院被公认为中国顶尖的综合医院之一，长期位居全国医院排名榜首。作为北京协和医学院（中国顶尖医学院）的附属医院，协和以严格的学术标准和处理疑难、罕见、复杂病例的能力著称。

Key Strengths

优势特色

  • Rheumatology & Immunology — Nationally recognized center for autoimmune and connective tissue diseases.
  • 风湿免疫科 — 全国公认的自身免疫病和结缔组织病中心。
  • Neurology — Leading programs in neurodegenerative disease, epilepsy, and neuromuscular disorders.
  • 神经内科 — 在神经退行性疾病、癫痫和神经肌肉疾病方面的领先项目。
  • Endocrinology — PUMCH's endocrinology department is one of the oldest and most respected in China.
  • 内分泌科 — 协和医院内分泌科是中国历史最悠久、最受尊敬的内分泌学科之一。
  • Rare & Complex Diseases — PUMCH serves as a national referral center for diagnostic dilemmas and rare conditions.
  • 疑难罕见病 — 协和医院是全国疑难杂症和罕见病的转诊中心。

PUMCH's International Medical Department provides services for foreign patients. YuanU can coordinate specialist consultations at PUMCH for international patients.

Peking University People's Hospital (PKUPH)

北京大学人民医院

Peking University People's Hospital is a Grade-A tertiary comprehensive hospital affiliated with Peking University Health Science Center. Founded in 1918, it is one of the earliest hospitals established by the Chinese government with Western medical training. The hospital has developed strong capabilities across multiple specialties and offers international medical services.

北京大学人民医院是北京大学医学部附属的三级甲等综合医院。始建于 1918 年，是中国最早由政府建立的采用西方医学训练的医院之一。医院在多个专科领域形成了强大实力，并提供国际医疗服务。

Key Strengths

优势特色

  • Orthopedics — One of China's leading orthopedic centers, with sub-specialties in spine, joint, sports medicine, and trauma.
  • 骨科 — 中国领先的骨科中心之一，设有脊柱、关节、运动医学和创伤等亚专科。
  • Hematology — National key discipline with strengths in leukemia, lymphoma, and bone marrow transplantation.
  • 血液科 — 国家重点学科，在白血病、淋巴瘤和骨髓移植方面具有优势。
  • Ophthalmology — Comprehensive eye care including retinal surgery, glaucoma, and cataract.
  • 眼科 — 综合眼科服务，包括视网膜手术、青光眼和白内障。
  • Thoracic Surgery — Minimally invasive thoracic and lung surgery programs.
  • 胸外科 — 微创胸外和肺部手术项目。

PKUPH offers international medical services. YuanU can coordinate specialist consultations at PKUPH for international patients.

北大人民医院提供国际医疗服务。远游可为国际患者协调北大人民医院的专家门诊。

§ How We Coordinate § 协调流程

From Inquiry to Follow-Up — Our Coordination Process

从咨询到跟进 — 我们的协调流程

01

Case Assessment

病情评估

You share your medical records and describe your needs. Our team reviews your case to understand which specialty and hospital would be the best fit.

您分享病历资料并说明需求。我们的团队审阅您的病情，确定最合适的专科和医院。

02

Hospital & Specialist Matching

医院与专家匹配

Based on the assessment, we identify the most appropriate hospital and specialist from our network, and present you with a recommendation.

根据评估结果，我们从网络中为您匹配最合适的医院和专家，并提供推荐方案。

03

Appointment Coordination

预约协调

We coordinate with the hospital to secure an appointment aligned with your travel schedule. All logistics — timing, department, documentation — are handled by our team.

我们与医院协调，安排与您行程匹配的预约。所有后勤——时间、科室、资料——均由我们的团队处理。

04

In-Person Accompaniment

到院陪同

A YuanU coordinator meets you at the hospital, handles check-in, provides bilingual interpretation throughout your visit, and ensures smooth communication.

远游协调员在医院迎接您，协助挂号，全程提供双语翻译，确保沟通顺畅。

After your visit, we provide translated medical summaries, coordinate any additional tests or prescriptions, and arrange follow-up appointments if needed.

就诊结束后，我们提供翻译的医疗摘要，协调后续检查或处方，并根据需要安排复诊。
