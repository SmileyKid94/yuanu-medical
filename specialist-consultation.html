§ How It Works § 服务流程

How Specialist Consultation Coordination Works

专科会诊协调流程

01

Pre-Trip Medical Communication

行前病情沟通

You submit your medical records, imaging reports, and a description of your concerns. Our coordinator reviews your materials and prepares a bilingual summary for the specialist.

您提交病历、影像报告和病情说明。我们的协调员审阅资料，为接诊专家准备中英双语摘要。

02

Specialty & Specialist Matching

匹配专科与专家

Based on your condition, we identify the most appropriate specialty and recommend specialists at Associate Chief Physician level or above at Beijing's Grade-A tertiary hospitals.

根据您的病情，我们确定最合适的专科方向，并推荐北京三甲医院副主任及以上级别的专家。

03

Appointment Booking

预约门诊

We coordinate with the hospital to secure an appointment at the International Medical Department (IMD) or the specialist's outpatient clinic, aligned with your travel schedule.

我们与医院协调，在国际医疗部或专家门诊为您预约，与您到北京的时间匹配。

04

In-Clininic Bilingual Accompaniment

到诊陪同翻译

A YuanU coordinator accompanies you to the hospital, provides bilingual interpretation throughout the consultation, and ensures clear communication between you and the physician.

远游协调员陪同您到院就诊，全程提供双语翻译，确保您与医生之间的沟通清晰顺畅。

05

Post-Consultation Follow-Up

诊后跟进

After the visit, we provide a translated summary of the consultation, coordinate any additional tests or prescriptions, and assist with follow-up scheduling if needed.

就诊结束后，我们提供诊次翻译摘要，协调后续检查或处方，并根据需要协助安排复诊。

§ Service Boundary § 服务边界

Important: Understanding Our Role

重要说明：了解我们的角色

YuanU is a medical-coordination service company, not a medical institution.

远游是医疗协调服务公司，非医疗机构。

  • All diagnoses, examinations, and treatments are provided independently by licensed physicians at cooperating hospitals.
  • YuanU does not provide medical opinions, make clinical recommendations, or guarantee specific clinical outcomes.
  • YuanU does not speak for or represent any hospital. We coordinate logistics, translation, and scheduling on behalf of the patient.
  • Specialist availability is subject to hospital scheduling; we coordinate based on availability but cannot guarantee specific dates or physicians.
  • 所有诊断、检查和治疗均由合作医院的执业医师独立提供。
  • 远游不出具医学意见、不做临床推荐、不就特定临床结果做承诺。
  • 远游不代表任何医院发言。我们为患者协调后勤、翻译和排期。
  • 专家出诊时间以医院排班为准，我们根据可用性协调，但无法保证特定日期或特定专家。
