§ 1 · Overview § 1 · 概述

1. Common sleep disorders

1. 睡眠障碍常见类型

Sleep disorders encompass a wide range of conditions. The descriptions below are for general reference only and are not a substitute for professional evaluation by a licensed physician.

睡眠障碍涵盖多种类型。以下描述仅供一般性参考，不能替代执业医师的专业评估。

Common categories

常见类别

  • Insomnia: difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep, or waking too early; can be short-term or chronic.
  • 失眠（Insomnia）：入睡困难、维持睡眠困难或过早醒来；可分为短期失眠与慢性失眠。
  • Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA): repeated upper airway obstruction during sleep, often accompanied by snoring and daytime sleepiness.
  • 阻塞性睡眠呼吸暂停（OSA）：睡眠中反复出现上气道阻塞，常伴有打鼾和日间嗜睡。
  • Narcolepsy: excessive daytime sleepiness, cataplexy, sleep paralysis, and disrupted nighttime sleep.
  • 发作性睡病（Narcolepsy）：日间过度嗜睡、猝倒发作、睡眠瘫痪以及夜间睡眠片段化。
  • Restless Legs Syndrome (RLS): uncomfortable sensations in the legs with an urge to move, typically worsening in the evening.
  • 不宁腿综合征（RLS）：腿部不适感伴强烈活动欲望，通常在傍晚或夜间加重。
  • Circadian Rhythm Disorders: misalignment between the internal body clock and the external environment, including delayed sleep-wake phase disorder and shift-work disorder.
  • 昼夜节律紊乱（Circadian Rhythm Disorders）：体内生物钟与外部环境失同步，包括睡眠-觉醒时相延迟障碍和轮班工作障碍。

Polysomnography (sleep study)

睡眠监测（Polysomnography）

Polysomnography is a comprehensive overnight recording conducted in a sleep laboratory. It simultaneously monitors brain waves (EEG), oxygen saturation, heart rate, breathing patterns, eye movements, and limb movements. The results help physicians understand the nature and severity of a sleep disorder.

多导睡眠监测（PSG）是在睡眠实验室进行的整夜综合记录，同步监测脑电波（EEG）、血氧饱和度、心率、呼吸模式、眼球运动和肢体运动。监测结果有助于医师了解睡眠障碍的性质与程度。

Note: YuanU does not interpret sleep study results. All clinical interpretation is provided independently by the attending physician at the hospital.

说明：远游不解读睡眠监测结果。所有临床解读均由医院主诊医师独立完成。

§ 2 · Why Beijing § 2 · 为何选择北京

2. Beijing's strengths in sleep medicine

2. 北京在睡眠医学领域的优势

Beijing is home to some of China's most established sleep medicine centers, drawing on the depth of its tertiary hospital system. International patients seeking evaluation of sleep disorders can access the following institutions through YuanU coordination:

北京拥有国内最成熟的睡眠医学中心集群，依托三甲医院体系的深厚资源。国际患者可通过远游协调，就诊于以下机构：

PKUPH Sleep Center

PKUPH 睡眠中心

Peking University People's Hospital operates one of Beijing's well-known sleep centers, with expertise in polysomnography and comprehensive sleep disorder evaluation.

北京大学人民医院设有北京知名的睡眠中心，在多导睡眠监测与睡眠障碍综合评估方面经验丰富。

Chaoyang Hospital Sleep Respiratory Center

朝阳医院睡眠呼吸中心

Beijing Chaoyang Hospital's Sleep and Respiratory Center is recognized for its focus on sleep-related breathing disorders, including obstructive sleep apnea.

北京朝阳医院睡眠呼吸中心在睡眠相关呼吸障碍（包括阻塞性睡眠呼吸暂停）领域享有声誉。

BFH Neurology Sleep Clinic

BFH 神经内科睡眠门诊

BFH-IMD (Beijing Friendship Hospital International Medical Department) offers neurology-led sleep outpatient services for insomnia, RLS, narcolepsy, and circadian rhythm disorders.

BFH-IMD（北京友谊医院国际医疗部）提供神经内科主导的睡眠门诊，覆盖失眠、不宁腿综合征、发作性睡病及昼夜节律紊乱等。

§ 4 · Process § 4 · 流程

4. From inquiry to sleep study report

4. 从初次咨询到睡眠监测报告

  1. Initial inquiry: You contact YuanU with a brief description of your sleep concerns. We review your information and suggest a suitable hospital and department.
  2. 初次咨询：您联系远游并简要描述睡眠方面的困扰。我们审核您的信息后，建议合适的医院和科室。
  3. Appointment confirmation: We coordinate the specialist appointment, confirm the date and time, and send you a pre-visit checklist (documents to bring, preparation notes).
  4. 预约确认：我们协调专科门诊预约，确认日期时间，并向您发送就诊前清单（需携带的材料、注意事项）。
  5. On-site visit: A YuanU coordinator accompanies you to the hospital, provides bilingual interpretation during the consultation, and assists with check-in and payment logistics.
  6. 到院就诊：远游协调员陪同您到院，在问诊过程中提供双语口译，并协助挂号及缴费事宜。
  7. Sleep study (if recommended): If the physician orders a polysomnography, we coordinate the overnight study scheduling and communicate preparation instructions.
  8. 睡眠监测（如医师建议）：如医师开具多导睡眠监测，我们协调整夜监测排期并传达检测准备须知。
  9. Report delivery & translation: After the hospital issues the report, we deliver it to you along with an English translation summary.
  10. 报告交付与翻译：医院出具报告后，我们将报告及英文翻译摘要一并交付给您。
  11. Follow-up coordination: If the physician recommends further evaluation or a follow-up visit, we coordinate the next appointment as needed.
  12. 后续协调：如医师建议进一步评估或复诊，我们按需协调下一次预约。
