Cardiology Consultation in Beijing — Coordinated by YuanU

心血管内科就医协调 — 远游医疗

YuanU coordinates cardiology consultations at leading tertiary hospitals in Beijing for international patients. Whether you need evaluation for chest symptoms, follow-up for a known cardiac condition, or cardiac imaging interpretation, we arrange specialist appointments, provide bilingual on-site support, translate medical records, and help you navigate the healthcare system with clarity and confidence.

远游为在京国际患者协调北京三甲医院心血管内科就医服务。无论您需要评估胸部症状、随访已知心脏问题，还是心脏影像解读，我们提供专科预约、双语陪诊、病历翻译，帮助您清晰、从容地对接医疗资源。

§ 1 · Common Conditions § 1 · 常见问题

1. Common cardiology visit scenarios

1. 心血管内科常见就诊场景

YuanU coordinates care across a range of common cardiology visit scenarios. The following list describes typical situations — it is not exhaustive and does not constitute medical advice.

远游协调覆盖多种常见心血管就诊场景。以下列举典型情况，非完整清单，亦不构成医学建议。

  • Coronary artery disease — coordination of specialist consultations for chest discomfort, known coronary disease follow-up, and post-intervention monitoring.
  • 冠心病 — 协调胸部不适就诊咨询、已知冠心病随访及术后监测。
  • Arrhythmia — coordinating evaluation for palpitations, syncope work-up, and atrial fibrillation management.
  • 心律失常 — 协调心悸评估、晕厥检查及房颤管理就诊。
  • Hypertension — coordination of visits for blood pressure assessment, medication review, and secondary hypertension screening.
  • 高血压 — 协调血压评估、用药复查及继发性高血压筛查就诊。
  • Valve disorders — coordinating evaluation and follow-up for aortic, mitral, and other valvular conditions.
  • 瓣膜病 — 协调主动脉瓣、二尖瓣等瓣膜病变的评估与随访。
  • Cardiomyopathy — coordination of specialist consultations for dilated, hypertrophic, or other cardiomyopathy evaluations.
  • 心肌病 — 协调扩张型、肥厚型及其他心肌病的专科就诊咨询。

Important note

重要提示

YuanU is a coordination service. We do not provide medical opinions, recommend specific examinations, or suggest clinical pathways. All decisions about what to check, how often, and which examinations to undergo are made by the licensed attending physician at the partner hospital.

远游是协调服务机构。我们不提供医学意见、不建议具体检查项目、不规划临床路径。所有关于检查内容、频率和方式的决策均由合作医院执业医师做出。

§ 2 · Why Beijing § 2 · 为何选择北京

2. Why Beijing for cardiology care

2. 北京心血管内科就医优势

Beijing hosts some of China's most prominent cardiology centers, with exceptionally high outpatient volumes and broad clinical experience. Key advantages for international patients include:

北京拥有全国知名的多个心血管中心，门诊量极高且临床经验丰富。对于国际患者的主要优势包括：

  • Fuwai Hospital (CAMS) — widely recognized as China's leading cardiovascular hospital, affiliated with the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences. Its cardiology department handles an exceptionally high volume of complex cases.
  • 阜外医院（中国医学科学院） — 全国公认的心血管领域领先医院，隶属中国医学科学院，心内科处理大量复杂病例。
  • Beijing Anzhen Hospital — a major cardiovascular center known for both clinical cardiology and cardiac surgery, with high procedural volume.
  • 北京安贞医院 — 大型心血管中心，以心脏内科与心脏外科见长，手术与操作量大。
  • PUMCH (Peking Union Medical College Hospital) — its cardiology department is among the most respected in the country, with comprehensive diagnostic capabilities.
  • PUMCH（北京协和医院） — 心内科是全国最受尊重的学科之一，具备全面诊断能力。
  • BFH (Beijing Friendship Hospital) — the cardiology department provides comprehensive outpatient and inpatient services.
  • BFH（北京友谊医院） — 心内科提供全面的门诊与住院服务。

Hospitals we coordinate with

我们协调合作的医院

YuanU coordinates care at multiple tertiary hospitals in Beijing, including Fuwai Hospital, Anzhen Hospital, PUMCH, BFH, and others depending on the patient's needs and physician availability.

远游在北京多家三甲医院提供协调服务，包括阜外医院、安贞医院、PUMCH（北京协和医院）、BFH（北京友谊医院）等，具体视患者需求与医师排班而定。

Hospital choice is based on clinical fit and availability, not on any commercial arrangement. YuanU does not receive referral fees from any hospital.

医院选择基于临床匹配度与可预约性，不涉及任何商业安排。远游不收取任何医院的转介费用。

§ 6 · Emergency Note § 6 · 紧急就医提示

6. Emergency note — we do not provide emergency services

6. 紧急就医提示 — 远游不提供急救服务

⚠ For medical emergencies, call 120 immediately

⚠ 如遇紧急情况，请立即拨打 120

YuanU is an administrative coordination service. We do not provide emergency medical care, do not operate an ambulance service, and cannot respond to acute cardiac events. If you or someone nearby is experiencing chest pain, difficulty breathing, fainting, or other acute symptoms, please call 120 (China's emergency number) or go to the nearest hospital emergency department immediately.

远游是行政协调服务机构。我们不提供急救医疗服务、不运营救护车、无法响应急性心脏事件。如您或身边的人出现胸痛、呼吸困难、晕厥或其他急性症状，请立即拨打 120（中国急救电话）或前往最近的医院急诊科。

§ 7 · Service Boundary § 7 · 服务边界

7. Service Boundary & disclaimer

7. 服务边界与免责

YuanU is a coordination service, not a medical institution

远游是协调服务机构，非医疗机构

YuanU provides administrative coordination and bilingual interpretation for cardiology visits at partner tertiary hospitals. We do not provide medical diagnoses, do not recommend specific examinations or treatment plans, and do not interpret clinical results. All medical decisions are made independently by licensed physicians at the partner hospitals.

远游为合作三甲医院的心血管就诊提供行政协调与双语翻译服务。我们不提供医学诊断、不建议具体检查或治疗方案、不解读临床结果。所有医学决策均由合作医院的执业医师独立做出。

明确排除项

  • 远游协调员不解读心电图结果、影像学发现、化验数值或病理报告。
  • YuanU does not recommend or endorse any specific medication, procedure, or clinical pathway.
  • 远游不建议或推荐任何特定药物、操作或临床路径。
