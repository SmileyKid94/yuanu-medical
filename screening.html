§ 4.4 · Why Beijing for screening § 4.4 · 为什么来北京体检

4. Why come to Beijing for screening

4. 为什么来北京做体检

Most patients we work with don't lack basic health checks at home — what they often lack is fast, integrated access to specialists, advanced examinations, and downstream care, all in one place. Beijing is one of the few cities in China where that combination sits inside a single hospital system.

大多数海外患者在所在国家做常规体检并不困难。真正缺的是 专家资源、检查资源与后续医疗资源的就近整合。北京是国内少数能在同一家医院体系内同时提供这三者的城市。

Specialist density

专家集中度

BFH·IMD's screening physicians sit in the same hospital as cardiology, gastroenterology, oncology, endocrinology, neurology and dozens of other specialty teams. If a screening finding warrants a specialist follow-up, that consultation often happens within the same trip — not three months later.

BFH·IMD 的体检医师与心血管、消化、肿瘤、内分泌、神经等几十个专科同在一家医院。体检中发现的问题，往往可以在同一次行程内完成专科会诊，而不是几个月后再约。

Examination breadth

检查可获得性

Tests that take weeks or months to schedule abroad — endoscopy, advanced imaging, sub-specialty ultrasounds, broad laboratory profiles — are typically available within days here, with most items grouped into a single morning.

在很多国家需要排几周甚至几个月的项目（胃肠镜、特殊影像、亚专科超声、特定实验室套餐），在 BFH·IMD 通常以天为单位排期，多数项目可在同一上午完成。

Downstream care, if it's needed

如有问题，闭环在同一家医院

If a finding needs further work-up, the same hospital can take you from screening → diagnostic workup → specialist consultation → treatment plan, without re-routing you to a different institution mid-way.

如果体检中发现异常，从体检 → 进一步检查 → 专科会诊 → 治疗方案，可以在同一家医院体系内顺接，不需要在不同机构之间反复辗转。

The point isn't that Beijing is the only city that can do this — it's that the integration is unusually tight here, and that's what makes a single-trip screening clinically useful for a patient flying in from abroad.

北京并不是唯一能做这件事的城市，但 集中度 在国内少有匹敌——这正是"一次行程完成体检"对海外患者真正有意义的原因。

§ 4.5 · How we design your screening § 4.5 · 我们如何为您设计体检方案

5. A screening is not a package — how we design yours

5. 体检不是买套餐 — 我们如何为您设计方案

A screening's value isn't measured in how many items it contains. It's measured in whether the items match your age, sex, family history and existing conditions. The conversation about which tests you actually need should happen before you arrive at the hospital — not on the morning of intake.

体检的价值不是项目越多越好，而是 这些项目是否匹配您的年龄、性别、家族史与既往病史。关于"您实际需要做哪些检查"的沟通，应当发生在 抵达医院之前，而不是体检当天临时决定。

A few common examples

几个常见情境

  • Family history of cancer — targeted oncology screening (specific biomarkers, focused imaging) is more informative than generic add-ons.
  • 有肿瘤家族史：针对性的肿瘤筛查（特定标志物、相应影像）比泛泛加项更有意义。
  • Hypertension or diabetes — cardiovascular and cerebrovascular risk assessment is often more useful than additional tumor markers.
  • 高血压或糖尿病：心脑血管风险评估往往比再加几项肿瘤标志物更有信息量。
  • Women — depending on age and reproductive history, breast and gynecological screening should be planned, not bundled by default.
  • 女性：根据年龄与生育史，乳腺与妇科专项检查应当被主动规划，而不是默认套餐里的附加项。
  • Conversely — some items routinely included in standard packages may carry no clinical value in your case. Removing them is part of the design too.
  • 反过来：标准套餐里的某些项目，对您可能没有临床价值。把它们去掉，同样是方案设计的一部分。

10. 体检相关常见问题

Q1 · Can I have just the screening without endoscopy?

Q1 · 我可以只做体检，不做胃肠镜吗？

Yes. Packages A / B / C / E do not include endoscopy. Endoscopy requires a dedicated exam day and bowel preparation; if you need it, we recommend Package D or a separate appointment (see the Endoscopy page).

可以。A / B / C / E 四档套餐均不含胃肠镜。胃肠镜需要单独的检查日与肠道准备，建议有需求的客户选 D 档或单独预约（详见 Endoscopy 专题页）。

Q2 · Do I need to fast? For how long?

Q2 · 体检需要空腹吗？要空腹多久？

Most items require 8–12 hours of fasting (no food or drink after 22:00 the night before). Specific fasting and medication rules are delivered by your YuanU coordinator in a personalized pre-visit checklist. Package D, which includes endoscopy, has additional bowel-cleansing requirements.

大多数项目要求 8–12 小时空腹（晚 22:00 后禁食水）。具体禁食 / 停药要求由远游协调员在行前发您专属准备清单。含胃肠镜的 D 档套餐另有肠道清洁要求。

Q3 · When are the reports ready? Can I wait in Beijing for the results?

Q3 · 报告什么时候出？我可以在北京等结果吗？

Most items return same-day results. Biopsy / pathology items take 5–7 business days. Genetic tests take 2–4 weeks. If you wish to wait in Beijing for a BFH physician's in-person review, plan about one week; if you prefer to return home sooner, YuanU coordinates the English summary remotely.

大部分项目当日出结果；活检 / 病理类项目需 5–7 个工作日；基因检测类需 2–4 周。如您希望在京等待并由 BFH 医师面诊解读，建议预留 1 周；如希望尽快回国，远游协调英文摘要远程发送。
