1. Who We Are

1. 我们是谁

YuanU Medical Concierge ("YuanU," "we," "us," "our") is the medical care coordination brand operated by Beijing YuanU International Travel Service Co., Ltd. We coordinate hospital appointments, on-site translation, itinerary planning, and invoice-related assistance for international clients receiving care in Beijing. We are not a hospital, clinic, or medical provider.

YuanU Medical Concierge（"远游"、"我们"）是由北京远游国际旅行社有限责任公司运营的医疗协调服务品牌。我们为在北京接受医疗服务的国际客户协调医院预约、现场翻译、行程规划及票据相关协助。我们不是医院、诊所或医疗服务提供者。

For any privacy-related question, please contact us by email (see Section 13). Our website does not publish a telephone number; all privacy inquiries are handled by email.

如有任何隐私相关问题，请通过电子邮件联系我们（见第 13 节）。我们的网站不公布电话号码，所有隐私咨询均通过电子邮件处理。

2. Information We Collect

2. 我们收集的信息

We collect only the information we need to respond to your inquiry and to coordinate your care visit.

我们仅收集回复您的咨询和协调您的就医行程所需的信息。

2.1 Information You Submit Through the Inquiry Form

2.1 您通过咨询表单提交的信息

When you complete the inquiry form on beijinghealthcarenavigation.com, we collect:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your country of residence
- A free-text description of the care you are seeking, which you may choose to include health-related details in

当您在 beijinghealthcarenavigation.com 填写咨询表单时，我们会收集以下信息：
- 您的姓名
- 您的电子邮件地址
- 您所在的国家/地区
- 您所寻求的医疗服务的自由文本描述，您可以选择在其中包含与健康相关的详情

You control how much health-related detail you enter. We ask you to share only what is necessary to help us understand your request.

您可以自行决定输入多少与健康相关的详细信息。我们请您仅分享帮助我们理解您的请求所必需的信息。

2.2 Information You Provide During Service

2.2 您在服务过程中提供的信息

Once we begin coordinating your visit, and only if you voluntarily provide it, we may receive:
- Prior medical records, imaging, or physician notes that you choose to send us to share with the treating hospital
- Passport or identity information required by the hospital for registration
- Travel dates and itinerary details relevant to scheduling
- Billing and invoice contact details

在我们开始协调您的行程后，且仅在您自愿提供的情况下，我们可能会收到：
- 您选择发送给我们以转交就诊医院的既往病历、影像资料或医生备注
- 医院注册所需的护照或身份信息
- 与预约安排相关的出行日期和行程详情
- 账单和发票联系信息

We do not request, and you should not send us, more health information than is needed for appointment coordination.

我们不会要求，您也不应向我们提供超出预约协调所需的更多健康信息。

2.3 自动收集的信息

When you visit the website, we may automatically receive limited technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, and pages viewed. See Section 8 (Cookies) for details.

当您访问本网站时，我们可能会自动接收有限的技术数据，如 IP 地址、浏览器类型、设备类型、来源页面和浏览页面。详情请参见第 8 节（Cookie）。

2.4 Sensitive / Special Category Data

2.4 敏感/特殊类别数据

Health-related information is treated as a special category of personal data under GDPR and as sensitive personal information under CPRA. We process it only with your explicit consent and only for the coordination purposes described in Section 3.

与健康相关的信息在 GDPR 下被视为特殊类别的个人数据，在 CPRA 下被视为敏感个人信息。我们仅在获得您的明确同意后，且仅用于第 3 节所述的协调目的对其进行处理。

3. 我们如何使用您的信息

We use your information strictly to:
- Respond to your inquiry
- Coordinate appointments with hospitals, including Beijing Friendship Hospital – International Medical Department (BFH-IMD), where we facilitate visits
- Arrange on-site interpretation and companion services
- Assist with travel and itinerary planning around your medical appointments
- Support invoice and receipt handling
- Communicate with you about the status of your case
- Meet legal, tax, and record-keeping obligations that apply to us

我们严格将您的信息用于以下目的：
- 回复您的咨询
- 与医院协调预约，包括我们协助就诊的北京友谊医院——国际医疗部（BFH-IMD）
- 安排现场翻译和陪同服务
- 协助围绕您的医疗预约进行差旅和行程规划
- 协助处理发票和收据
- 与您沟通您的案件进展状态
- 履行适用于我们的法律、税务和档案保存义务

我们不会出售您的个人信息。我们不会将您的信息用于行为广告。我们不会与数据经纪人或营销网络共享您的信息。

- Performance of a contract — to deliver the coordination services you have requested
- Legitimate interests — to operate and secure the website, and to respond to inquiries
- Legal obligation — for tax, accounting, and regulatory record-keeping

根据具体交互情况，我们依赖以下法律依据：

- 合同履行——用于提供您所请求的协调服务
- 合法利益——用于运营和保障网站安全，以及回复咨询
- 法律义务——用于税务、会计和监管档案保存

  • 投诉权——向您当地的数据保护机构（欧盟居民）或加利福尼亚州隐私保护机构（加利福尼亚州居民）投诉
