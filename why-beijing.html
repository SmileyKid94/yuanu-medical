Why Beijing为什么北京

Why Beijing — the honest case for choosing China's capital for medical care

为什么选择北京 — 区域医疗中枢的真实优势与边界

Beijing concentrates the highest density of Class-A tertiary hospitals and national clinical research centers in mainland China, covering virtually all major specialties. For international patients from Asia, the Middle East, the CIS, Africa and North America, Beijing offers a distinctive combination of specialist depth, breadth, cost position and geographic reach. This page sets out, using third-party-verifiable facts, what Beijing is good for — and what it is not.

北京聚集了中国大陆密度最高的三级甲等医院与国家级临床医学研究中心，覆盖几乎所有主要专科。对于来自亚洲、中东、独联体、非洲与北美的国际患者，北京在专家资源密度、专科广度、费用区间与地理便利之间提供了一个独特的组合。本页用第三方可查的事实，告诉您北京适合什么、不适合什么。

Beijing's medical resource density

北京医疗资源密度

Within mainland China's medical landscape, Beijing is one of the most concentrated cities for Class-A tertiary public general hospitals and tertiary specialty hospitals. National Clinical Research Centers, National Regional Medical Centers and National Medical Centers are densely distributed here, covering digestive, cardiovascular, oncology, neurology, respiratory and renal disciplines. Beijing is also one of the few mainland cities with multiple independent International Medical Departments, which means international patients can reach the same senior-attending physicians of public Class-A tertiary hospitals for appointments, consultations and report delivery — without entering the general outpatient flow.

This is the underlying reason YuanU anchored its first business line in Beijing rather than in another city — not a geographic preference, but a resource one.

在中国大陆的医疗版图中，北京是公立三级甲等综合医院与三级专科医院数量最为集中的城市之一，国家临床医学研究中心、国家区域医疗中心与国家医学中心在此密集分布，覆盖消化、心血管、肿瘤、神经、呼吸、肾脏等主要学科。北京同时是少数几个拥有多家独立国际医疗部的城市，这意味着海外患者可以在不进入普通门诊流程的前提下，与公立三甲医院的同一批主任医师级专家完成预约、就诊与报告交付。

这是远游把第一阶段业务锚定在北京、而非其他城市的根本原因 — 不是地理选择，是资源选择。

What Beijing is good for — and what it is not

海外患者来京的真实优势与限制

Choosing a city for medical care is not a marketing decision. We list both the real strengths Beijing offers international patients, and the real limitations you should plan around before you fly.

为医疗选择一座城市，不应该是一次营销决策。下面同时列出北京对国际患者的真实优势，以及您在出行前需要计入预算的真实限制。

Real strengths

真实优势

  • Relative accessibility of senior specialists. Through BFH·IMD and YuanU coordination, international patients can reach senior-attending-level specialists within a reasonable window — something that is not easy under self-service domestic appointment channels.
  • A complete specialty chain. Screening → endoscopy → imaging → pathology → multidisciplinary consultation → treatment can be completed inside one hospital system, avoiding repeated cross-hospital referrals.
  • Cost position relative to North America & Western Europe. Senior-attending consultations and examinations at comparable expertise levels are typically meaningfully lower than their North-American and Western-European counterparts. Actual ratios vary by item and exchange rate; YuanU does not publish percentage estimates or guarantees on its website.
  • Geographic reach. 5–6 hours of flight time covers Southeast Asia, Northeast Asia, the Middle East and the Russian Far East.
  • 触达资深专家的相对容易。通过 BFH·IMD 与远游协调，国际患者可以在合理时间窗口内见到主任医师级专家；这在国内自助挂号场景下并不容易。
  • 完整的专科链条。从体检 → 内镜 → 影像 → 病理 → 多学科会诊 → 治疗，在同一医院体系内完成，避免跨院反复转诊。
  • 费用相对欧美的优势。同等级别专家会诊与检查的费用，通常显著低于北美与西欧的对应水平；具体比例因项目与汇率而异，远游不在网站上估算或承诺。
  • 地理便利。5–6 小时飞行覆盖东南亚、东北亚、中东与俄远东。

Real limitations

真实限制

  • It is not a walk-in service. BFH·IMD senior-attending slots typically take 2–5 working days to confirm; during peak spring and autumn windows, lead times may extend to 1–2 weeks.
  • It operates on a public-hospital rhythm. BFH·IMD is the international department of a public hospital, primarily operating weekdays 8:00–17:00; routine outpatient care is not offered outside working hours.
  • We do not handle emergency cross-border medical evacuation. YuanU does not undertake critical-care international transport. If you are in an acute condition, please prioritise your country's local emergency services and professional cross-border medical evacuation providers.
  • Insurance direct billing is limited. BFH·IMD and YuanU currently do not accept most overseas commercial insurance for direct billing; in most cases, patients pay on-site and seek reimbursement from their insurer. [TBC: BFH·IMD currently accepted insurance direct-billing list (if any)]
  • Cross-language coverage has limits. Core languages are English and Chinese; French, Japanese and Russian are available by reservation; other languages require third-party medical interpretation booked at least 5 working days in advance.
  • Visa issuance is not in our hands. YuanU can provide an appointment confirmation letter to support your visa application, but visa issuance rests with the Chinese embassies and consulates abroad.
  • 不是即来即看。BFH·IMD 主任医师档期通常需要 2–5 个工作日预约确认；春秋两季档期紧张时可能延长至 1–2 周。
  • 公立医院的工作节奏。BFH·IMD 是公立医院的独立科室，工作日 8:00–17:00 为主；非工作时间不提供常规门诊。
  • 不接收急诊跨境转运。远游不承接重症急救国际转运业务；如您处于危急状态，请优先选择您所在国的本地急诊与跨境医疗转运专业机构。
  • 保险直付能力有限。BFH·IMD 与远游目前不接受多数海外商业保险直付；多数情况下需患者本人现场支付后向保险公司报销。[待确认：BFH·IMD 当前可接受的保险直付清单（若有）]
  • 跨语种限制。核心语言为英、中；法、日、俄通过预约支持；其他语种需至少 5 个工作日前预约第三方医学翻译。
