§ 1 · Overview § 1 · 概述

1. Common orthopedic consultation scenarios

1. 骨科常见就诊场景

International patients may seek orthopedic consultation in Beijing for a variety of musculoskeletal concerns. The following are common scenarios where YuanU can assist with coordination.

国际患者可能因各类骨骼肌肉问题来京寻求骨科就诊。以下是远游可协助协调的常见就诊场景。

Joint & Spine

关节与脊柱

  • Joint replacement evaluation: pre-surgical assessment for hip, knee, or shoulder replacement; second-opinion coordination.
  • 关节置换评估：髋关节、膝关节、肩关节置换术前评估；第二意见协调。
  • Spinal disorders: evaluation of lumbar disc herniation, spinal stenosis, scoliosis, and degenerative spinal conditions.
  • 脊柱疾病：腰椎间盘突出、椎管狭窄、脊柱侧弯及退行性脊柱疾病的评估。
  • Osteoporosis: bone density assessment and fracture risk evaluation for patients with recurrent fractures or long-term bone health concerns.
  • 骨质疏松：骨密度评估及反复骨折或长期骨骼健康关注患者的骨折风险评估。

Sports & Trauma

运动与创伤

  • Sports injuries: evaluation of ligament tears (ACL, PCL), meniscal injuries, rotator cuff damage, and tendon disorders.
  • 运动损伤：韧带撕裂（ACL、PCL）、半月板损伤、肩袖损伤及肌腱疾病的评估。
  • Fracture assessment: evaluation of acute or malunited fractures, including complex or periarticular fractures requiring specialist input.
  • 骨折评估：急性骨折或畸形愈合骨折的评估，包括需要专科意见的复杂骨折或关节周围骨折。
  • Arthroscopy: evaluation for arthroscopic procedures including diagnostic arthroscopy and arthroscopic surgery of knee, shoulder, ankle, or other joints.
  • 关节镜手术：关节镜手术的评估，包括诊断性关节镜及膝、肩、踝等关节的关节镜手术。

§ 6 · Post-Surgical Recovery § 6 · 术后康复支持

6. Post-surgical recovery support

6. 术后康复支持

For international patients undergoing orthopedic surgery in Beijing, YuanU can coordinate post-surgical recovery logistics.

对于在北京进行骨科手术的国际患者，远游可协调术后康复后勤支持。

Accommodation & Logistics

住宿与后勤

  • Recovery-friendly accommodation near the hospital, selected based on accessibility needs (elevator, ground floor, wheelchair access if needed).
  • 医院附近康复型住宿，根据行动需求选择（电梯、一楼、如需轮椅通道）。
  • Daily check-in coordination during early recovery period.
  • 术后早期恢复期间的每日签到协调。
  • Transportation to follow-up appointments.
  • 复诊交通安排。

Follow-Up & Physical Therapy

复诊与物理治疗

  • Follow-up visit scheduling with the operating surgeon.
  • 与主刀医师的复诊预约。
  • Physical therapy referral to qualified rehabilitation facilities.
  • 物理治疗转介至合格的康复机构。
  • Report translation for follow-up results before your departure.
  • 离境前复诊结果的报告翻译。

Note: YuanU coordinates the logistics of recovery — accommodation, transportation, follow-up scheduling, and therapy referrals. We do not provide nursing care, physical therapy, or any clinical services. All clinical decisions are made independently by the treating hospital.

说明：远游协调康复后勤——住宿、交通、复诊安排及治疗转介。我们不提供护理、物理治疗或任何临床服务。所有临床决策由就诊医院独立做出。
