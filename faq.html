Q1

How do I start?

我如何开始？

Fill in the inquiry form on the Contact page, or send an email to contact@yuanu.com. Within one business day (Beijing working hours, 09:00–18:00 CST), a YuanU Patient Coordinator will reply in your preferred language (English, Chinese, French, Japanese or Russian).

填写 Contact 页 的咨询表，或发送邮件至 contact@yuanu.com。北京工作时间内（CST 9:00–18:00）1 个工作日内由一名 YuanU Patient Coordinator 用您偏好的语言（英 / 中 / 法 / 日 / 俄）首次回复。

Once we receive your initial intent, the coordinator will request your medical records and return a coordination plan within 2–5 business days (subject to BFH·IMD physician availability).

收到您的初步意向后，协调员会请您提供既往病历，并在 2–5 个工作日内出具协调方案（受 BFH·IMD 医生档期影响）。

Q2

Can YuanU help with my China medical visa?

你们能办医疗签证吗？

YuanU can assist with the application for a Chinese medical visa, but cannot apply on your behalf and cannot guarantee that a visa will be granted. Specifically:

远游可以协助签证申请，但不能代办、不能保证签发。具体来说：

  • What we can do: After you confirm an appointment, we issue a formal appointment confirmation letter that may serve as a supporting document for your Chinese medical-visa application (M / Q1 or other visa types, subject to embassy/consulate requirements);
  • What we cannot do: We cannot issue a visa invitation letter, cannot submit a visa application on your behalf, and cannot influence visa-approval decisions;
  • For visa applications, please contact the Chinese embassy or consulate in your home country directly regarding the specific visa type, required documents and processing time.
  • 远游能做：在您确认预约后出具正式的预约确认函（appointment confirmation letter），该函可作为您向中国驻外使领馆申请医疗签证（M / Q1 等签证类型，以使领馆要求为准）的支持文件之一；
  • 远游不能做：不能签发签证邀请函（visa invitation letter）、不能代您提交签证申请、不能影响签证审批结果；
  • 签证申请请直接联系您所在国的中国驻外使领馆，咨询具体签证类型、所需材料与办理时长。
Q3

What does the coordination fee include?

协调服务费包含什么？

YuanU uses a three-tier pricing model: $499 (Essential — initial consultation / screening) / $1,999 (Specialist — specialist consultation / diagnostic workup) / $3,999 (Complex Care — multi-specialty / surgery coordination). The applicable tier is confirmed before service begins. Includes:

远游采用三档定价：$499（基础档 — 初步问诊/体检）/ $1,999（专科档 — 专科会诊/诊断检查）/ $3,999（复杂专科档 — 多专科/手术协调）。适用档位在服务开始前确认。含：

  • Pre-visit consultation + medical-record review + preparation checklist;
  • Appointment coordination and physician-schedule communication;
  • Airport transfer / hotel transfer (within the hospital vicinity);
  • Full in-room accompaniment on the day of screening or examination;
  • Chinese-English medical interpretation in clinical settings (other languages billed separately);
  • English summary translation of the report (standard version);
  • One round of remote follow-up after you return home.
  • 行前咨询 + 病历审核 + 准备清单制定；
  • 预约协调与医生档期沟通；
  • 接送机 / 酒店接送（医院附近）；
  • 体检 / 检查日全程院内陪诊；
  • 临床场景中英双语翻译（其他语种另计）；
  • 报告英文摘要翻译（标准版）；
  • 回国后 1 轮远程随访。

Does not include:

不含：

  • Hospital-side medical fees (collected directly by the hospital);
  • Hospital add-ons such as anesthesia / biopsy / pathology / endoscopic therapeutic interventions (billed by the hospital per its rules);
  • Flights / hotels / visa application fees / personal expenses;
  • Third-language professional medical interpretation (book at least 5 business days in advance; billed hourly);
  • Additional remote follow-up beyond the first round (billed hourly).
  • 医院端医疗费（医院直收）；
  • 麻醉 / 活检 / 病理 / 内镜下治疗等医院附加项（医院按规则计费）；
  • 机票 / 酒店 / 签证申请费 / 个人消费；
  • 第三方语种专业医学翻译（5 工作日前预约，按小时计）；
  • 超出 1 轮的额外远程随访（按小时计）。
Q4

What medical records should I send before my visit?

行前需要寄哪些病历？

After the inquiry is confirmed, we suggest sending the following materials within one week via encrypted email or a secure file link (whichever you have):

建议在咨询确认后 1 周内通过加密邮件 / 安全文件链接发送以下材料（如有）：

  • Recent 3-month clinical summary (chief complaint, diagnosis, treatment course);
  • Prior examination reports: blood counts, biochemistry, tumor markers, imaging (CT / MRI / ultrasound);
  • Imaging in DICOM format preferred (PACS disc or download link both acceptable);
  • Prior endoscopy / pathology reports (with slide numbers, if possible);
  • Current medication list (brand name + generic name + dose + duration);
  • Allergy history (drug / food / anesthesia);
  • Surgical history.
  • 近 3 个月病历摘要（含主诉、诊断、治疗经过）；
  • 既往检查报告：血常规、生化、肿瘤标志物、影像（CT / MRI / 超声）；
  • 影像 DICOM 文件优先（PACS 光盘 / 网络下载链接均可）；
  • 既往内镜 / 病理报告（含切片号，如可能）；
  • 当前用药清单（含商品名 + 通用名 + 剂量 + 用药时长）；
  • 过敏史（药物 / 食物 / 麻醉）；
  • 既往手术史。

Materials may be sent in batches. If you're unsure whether a specific item is needed, your coordinator will assess based on your scenario and let you know. Send via encrypted email or secure file link only.

资料可分次发送；如您不确定某项是否需要，由协调员根据您的具体场景判断后告知。仅通过加密邮件或安全文件链接发送。

Q5

What language services do you provide?

提供哪些语言服务？

  • Chinese-English bilingual: included by default, covering all clinical and administrative communication;
  • French, Japanese, Russian: available via BFH·IMD's multilingual reception combined with YuanU coordinator scheduling support;
  • Other languages (e.g., Arabic, Spanish, Portuguese, Korean): require at least 5 business days' advance booking; YuanU coordinates a third-party professional medical interpreter, billed hourly and invoiced separately.
  • 中英双语：默认包含，覆盖所有临床场景与行政沟通；
  • 法、日、俄：可通过 BFH·IMD 的多语种接待 + 远游协调员预约支持；
  • 其他语种（如阿拉伯语、西班牙语、葡萄牙语、韩语等）：需至少 5 个工作日前预约，由远游协调第三方专业医学翻译；费用按小时计、单独发票。

Scope: YuanU's interpretation service covers clinical settings (consultations with physicians, on-site during examinations, report explanation) and necessary administrative communication only. It does not cover sightseeing, business negotiation, or personal-matter accompaniment.

范围说明：远游的翻译服务仅覆盖临床场景（医师面谈、检查现场、报告解读）与必要的行政沟通；不含旅游观光、商务谈判、私人事务等场景的陪同翻译。

Q6

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

如何取消或改期？

  • ≥ 7 days before appointment: full reschedule or full refund of YuanU's coordination fee; whether the hospital side refunds follows the hospital's own rules;
  • 3–7 days before appointment: rescheduling possible; if cancelled, 50% of YuanU's coordination fee is deducted;
  • < 3 days before appointment: coordination fee is non-refundable; hospital-side fees follow the hospital's own rules;
  • Fees already incurred on the hospital side (e.g., pre-procedure assessment, anesthesia already drawn): follow the hospital's own rules.
  • 预约日前 ≥ 7 天：可全额改期或退款（远游协调服务费）；医院端是否退款按医院规则；
  • 预约日前 3–7 天：可改期；如取消，远游协调服务费扣 50%；
  • 预约日前 < 3 天：协调服务费不予退还；医院端按医院规则；
  • 医院端已发生的费用（如已做术前评估 / 已用麻醉药）：按医院规则。

Rescheduling typically requires BFH·IMD to reopen physician availability and may take 1–2 weeks. In cases of medical urgency or force majeure (natural disaster, visa refusal, etc.), the coordinator will negotiate with the hospital on a case-by-case basis. Hospital-side fees follow the hospital's own cancellation policy, which YuanU does not control.

改期通常需要 BFH·IMD 重新安排医生档期，可能需要 1–2 周；急症 / 不可抗力（如自然灾害、签证拒签）情况下协调员将与医院端协商，按个案处理。医院端费用按医院自身的取消政策执行，远游无权代为决定。

Q7

What happens if my biopsy / report shows something abnormal?

如果活检结果异常怎么办？

Handling pathway for abnormal results:

异常结果的处理路径：

  1. BFH·IMD attending physician explanation in Beijing: once an abnormal result is available, the attending physician will arrange a 30–45 minute explanation session (remote via the hospital's video system, or in person). YuanU coordinates scheduling and interpretation.
  2. Further examination / multidisciplinary consultation: if further imaging, biopsy or a specialist consultation is recommended, YuanU assists with the appointment and coordination.
  3. Report transfer to your home-country physician: with your written authorization, YuanU can send the report and English summary via an encrypted channel to a physician you designate, supporting follow-up after you return home.
  1. BFH·IMD 主诊医生在京解读：通常在异常结果出来后，主诊医生会安排 30–45 分钟的解读会（远程或现场，远程通过医院视频系统）。远游协调员协调档期与翻译。
  2. 进一步检查 / 多学科会诊衔接：如医师建议进一步影像 / 活检 / 专科会诊，远游协助预约与协调。
  3. 报告传递给您本国医生：经您书面授权后，远游可将报告 + 英文摘要通过加密渠道发送给您指定的本国医师，便于您回国后继续随访。

To be clear: YuanU coordinators do not interpret any abnormal findings. All clinical judgments are made independently by physicians at BFH·IMD and partnering hospitals.

明确：远游协调员不解读任何异常结果。所有医学判断由 BFH·IMD 及合作医院的执业医师独立完成。

Q8

Can you help me access my medical records after I return home?

回国后如何获取病历？

Yes. With your written authorization in advance:

可以。在您书面授权前提下：

  • YuanU can retrieve the English clinical summary and the original Chinese reports (including pathology / imaging) issued by BFH·IMD;
  • They will be delivered to you or your designated home-country physician via encrypted email or a secure file-transfer link;
  • The standard version (1 retrieval + 1 delivery) is included in the coordination fee; additional retrievals are billed hourly;
  • Imaging DICOM files are made available per hospital policy (some via download link, others by on-site disc copy).
  • 远游可代取 BFH·IMD 出具的英文病历摘要 + 中文原报告（含病理 / 影像报告）；
  • 通过加密邮件 / 安全文件传输链接发送给您本人 / 您指定的本国医师；
  • 标准版（1 次代取 + 1 次传递）含在协调服务费内；额外代取按小时计；
  • 影像 DICOM 文件可按医院规定提供（部分可下载链接、部分需现场拷贝光盘）。

If you need pathology slides shipped to your home-country pathology department for a second opinion, YuanU can assist with the hospital's slide-release procedure and cross-border logistics (handling fee per hospital policy + actual logistics cost).

如您需要切片寄送给本国病理科二次会诊，远游可协助办理院方调阅与跨境寄送手续（手续费按医院规定 + 物流实际费用计）。

Q9

How can my home-country doctor follow up with the BFH·IMD physician?

我国内的医生如何与 BFH 医师进一步沟通？

If you would like your home-country physician to communicate with the BFH·IMD attending physician at a professional level, there are the following paths:

如您希望您本国的医师与 BFH·IMD 主诊医师进行专业层面的沟通，有以下路径：

  • Written consultation (recommended): your home-country physician prepares specific questions (English or Chinese), which the YuanU coordinator forwards to the BFH·IMD physician; the BFH physician replies in writing within 1–2 weeks;
  • Remote video consultation: YuanU coordinates schedules and the communication time between the two physicians; the hospital bills its consultation fee separately according to its own schedule;
  • YuanU does not participate in the professional discussion between physicians; YuanU coordinates scheduling, document handover, and bilingual interpretation when needed.
  • 书面会诊（推荐）：您本国医师准备具体问题（英文 / 中文），通过远游协调员转交 BFH·IMD 医师，1–2 周内由 BFH 医师以书面形式回复；
  • 远程视频会诊：远游协调档期 + 双方医师沟通时间；医院按其专家会诊费用单独计费；
  • 远游不参与医师之间的专业讨论；远游负责协调档期、传递文件、必要时双语翻译。
