What We Do

We coordinate — we do not diagnose or prescribe. Our role is to make Beijing's leading hospitals accessible and navigable for international patients, families and corporate clients.

Appointment Coordination

English booking with international medical departments, department-level scheduling, interpreter arrangement for consultations and follow-ups.

Executive Health Check-ups

VIP corridor access at BFH-IMD, private examination rooms, English-language reports, delivery to your home country or referring physician.

Specialist Consultations

Second-opinion visits and follow-up care across cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, gastroenterology, ophthalmology, dentistry and traditional Chinese medicine.

Insurance Direct-Billing

Verification of your coverage against BFH-IMD's confirmed direct-billing list, pre-authorization coordination and hospital-side billing office liaison.

Post-Treatment Recovery

Recovery accommodation arrangements in Beijing, follow-up appointment scheduling, medication pickup coordination and discharge documentation support.

Responsible Companion Support

Non-medical companion service for elderly or unaccompanied patients: hospital transfers, in-room support, meal coordination and daily check-ins.

Hospitals We Coordinate With

We coordinate visits at three of Beijing's leading Grade 3A tertiary hospitals. Each has a dedicated international medical department or English-support pathway.

BFH-IMD

Beijing Friendship Hospital · International Medical Department

Our primary coordination site. English-speaking staff, 30+ international insurers accepted with direct billing, VIP health check-up corridor, seven specialty clusters.

PUMCH

Peking Union Medical College Hospital

Case-by-case coordination for complex specialist consultations. Nationally ranked top hospital, particularly for rare diseases and multi-disciplinary reviews.

PKUPH

Peking University People's Hospital

Case-by-case coordination for orthopedics, hematology and trauma care. Strong reputation in surgical specialties and post-operative recovery pathways.

We coordinate visits at these hospitals; we are not their partner, agent, or authorized representative. All medical decisions remain with the hospital's licensed physicians.
