北京的医生在那里。

For international patients, the bottleneck of Beijing healthcare is rarely the physician — it is everything between the first inquiry and the consulting room: language, hospital workflow, payment routes, the right department, the right specialist tier, the report afterwards. YuanU is the licensed Beijing company that builds and walks that path with you, in long-term partnership with Beijing Friendship Hospital's International Medical Department (BFH·IMD).

对海外患者来说，北京医疗的瓶颈很少是医生本身，而是从第一封咨询邮件到诊室之间的一整段路——语言、医院流程、支付路径、对的科室、对的专家级别、检查后的报告衔接。远游是一家在北京持照运营的公司，与北京友谊医院国际医疗部（BFH·IMD）长期协作，把这段路一步一步陪您走通。

The question we get most 最常被问到的问题

Why not just contact the hospital directly?

为什么不直接联系医院？

You can. The hospital exists, the department exists, the email address is public. But hospitals run on hospital time, in Mandarin, and a senior specialist's calendar is not what the front desk manages. What looks like one email is in practice a 6–8 week chain across departments — and most international patients never see where it broke.

完全可以。医院在那里，科室在那里，邮箱也是公开的。但医院按医院的节奏、用中文运转，资深专家的档期不在前台手里。看上去是发一封邮件，实际是跨多个部门、6–8 周的协调链——而大多数海外患者根本看不到链条在哪一段断了。

A YuanU coordinator does what the hospital cannot do for you from inside its own walls: triage your records before booking, match them to the right department and the right specialist tier, walk through the day with you in your language, and translate the report afterwards — including the part the doctor did not have time to dictate.

远游协调员做的，是医院从内部做不到的那部分：在挂号之前先把您的病历过一遍，匹配到对的科室、对的专家级别；当天用您的语言陪您走完流程；事后把报告翻译给您——包括医生没时间口述的那一部分。

That is the difference between getting an appointment and being correctly placed in a clinical pathway.

这就是"挂上一个号"和"被正确放进一条临床路径"的区别。

Who we are 我们是谁

A licensed coordinator.
Not a medical institution.

一家持照运营的医疗协调服务公司，
不是医疗机构

YuanU exists for one specific reason: international patients who need Beijing-grade specialist care should not have to negotiate with the Chinese hospital system in Chinese. We are a licensed medical coordination service company (medical concierge) based in Beijing, working in long-term partnership with BFH·IMD and other Beijing tertiary hospitals. We do not provide medical care. We make Beijing medicine reachable.

远游存在的理由很具体：需要北京级专家诊疗的海外患者，不应该被迫用中文和中国医院系统打交道。我们是一家在北京持照运营的医疗协调服务公司（medical concierge），与北京友谊医院国际医疗部及其他北京三甲医院长期协作。我们不做诊疗，我们让北京医疗对海外患者"够得着"。

Each YuanU patient is paired with a dedicated coordinator — bilingual, non-clinical liaison, single point of contact — from first inquiry to follow-up after you fly home.

每一位远游患者都由一名专属协调员负责——双语、非临床联络人、单一对接人——从第一封咨询邮件到您回国后的随访，全程由同一个人陪您走完。

All diagnoses, examinations and treatments are provided independently by the attending physicians. YuanU does not issue any medical opinions. 所有的诊断、检查与治疗均由医院的执业医师独立完成。远游不出具任何医学意见。

Why YuanU 为什么是远游

Four facts, not four adjectives.

用 4 条事实回答"为什么是远游"

01

Licensed and clearly scoped

持照运营、定位清晰

YuanU holds a travel-agency operating license issued by the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture and Tourism (No. L-BJ-CJ00262), with inbound medical coordination as our primary business. Corporate governance and invoicing are compliant and auditable. We tell every client plainly: we coordinate; we do not provide clinical care.

远游持有北京市文化和旅游局颁发的旅行社业务经营许可证（L-BJ-CJ00262），主营入境医疗协调；公司治理与开票口径合规、可审计。我们明确告诉每一位客户：我们做协调，不做诊疗。

02

Long-term relationship with BFH·IMD

与北京友谊医院 IMD 的长期协作

BFH·IMD is an independent clinical department (not a "VIP window" bolted onto general outpatient flow), with multilingual reception in English, French, Japanese and Russian. YuanU coordinators have established stable hospital-side procedures for appointments, in-room accompaniment and report delivery.

BFH·IMD 是独立科室（不是普通门诊的"贵宾窗口"），提供英、法、日、俄等多语言接待。远游协调员在医院端的预约、陪诊与报告交付流程上已建立稳定的工作机制。

03

One coordinator. End to end.

专人对接、贯穿全程

Every international patient is assigned a single YuanU Patient Coordinator — bilingual, non-clinical liaison — who stays with you from the first email through post-return follow-up. No handovers, no information gaps. The coordinator does not answer medical questions in place of the physician, but accurately translates your questions to the doctor and the doctor's words back to you.

每一位国际患者由一名 YuanU Patient Coordinator（双语、非临床联络人）从第一封邮件到回国后随访全程负责，避免被多人对接、信息断点。协调员不替代医生回答医学问题，但会准确把医生的话翻译给您、把您的问题翻译给医生。

04

Clear boundaries. Transparent pricing.

边界清楚、报价透明

The hospital-side medical cost and YuanU's coordination fee are presented as two separate line items before you travel. YuanU's coordination fee is calculated as 15%–25% of the hospital-side medical cost (a reference range that depends on package complexity, accompaniment hours, and whether post-visit follow-up is included). No bundled pricing. No hidden margins.

医院端医疗费与远游协调服务费在行前以 两条独立条目 分别给出；远游协调服务费按医院端医疗费用的 15%–25% 区间核算（基于套餐复杂度、陪诊时长、是否含术后随访）；不存在"打包价"、不存在隐藏分成。

How it works 协调流程

From inquiry to post-return follow-up — six steps.

从咨询到回国后随访，共六步

Step 01 · Inquire 第 01 步 · 咨询

You submit an inquiry

您提交咨询

Submit via the form or by email. A YuanU coordinator replies within one business day (Beijing time).

通过表单或邮件提交咨询。1 个工作日内（北京时间）由远游协调员回复。

Step 02 · Records 第 02 步 · 病历

We collect your medical records

收集您的既往病历

The coordinator asks for your recent 3-month clinical summary, prior examination reports, medication list and allergy history.

远游协调员请您提供近 3 个月病程、既往检查报告、用药清单与过敏史。

Step 03 · Plan 第 03 步 · 方案

Coordination plan + cost estimate

出具协调方案与费用预估

YuanU confirms physician availability with BFH·IMD and returns a coordination plan, service fee, and hospital-side cost estimate within 2–5 business days.

远游与 BFH·IMD 确认医生档期，给出协调方案、服务费与医院端预估费用（2–5 个工作日）。

Step 04 · Confirm 第 04 步 · 确认

Appointment confirmation letter

出具预约确认函

After you confirm the plan, YuanU issues an appointment confirmation letter — which may be used to support your application for a Chinese medical visa.

您确认方案后远游出具预约确认函，可用于支持中国医疗签证申请。

Step 05 · Care 第 05 步 · 就诊

On-site care & in-room accompaniment

院内陪诊与双语翻译

After arrival, BFH conducts the examination or consultation; the YuanU coordinator provides in-room accompaniment and bilingual interpretation throughout.

抵达后 BFH 完成检查 / 会诊；远游协调员全程院内陪诊与双语翻译。

Step 06 · Follow-up 第 06 步 · 随访

Report delivery + 1 remote follow-up

报告交付与回国随访

Once the report is ready, YuanU coordinates delivery of an English summary → one round of remote follow-up after you return home is included in standard coordination.

报告就绪后远游协调英文摘要交付，回国后 1 轮远程随访（含在标准协调服务中）。
