Patient Support · §6 患者支持 · §6

From your first inquiry to follow-up after you fly home — one dedicated YuanU Patient Coordinator

从第一封邮件到回国后随访 — 远游患者协调员全程对接

YuanU does not provide medical services, but provides every administrative and communication function that wraps around them. This page lays out, item by item, the specific support work we do — and the complete timeline from inquiry through follow-up after you return home.

远游不提供医疗服务，但提供围绕医疗服务的全部行政与沟通协调。本页详细列出每一项具体支持工作 + 您从咨询到回国后随访的完整时间线。

YuanU Patient Coordinator  ·  bilingual · non-clinical liaison · single point of contact 远游患者协调员  ·  双语 · 非临床联络人 · 单一对接窗口

What we do · 8 services 服务清单 · 8 项

Eight defined support services

8 项支持服务清单

Each service below states the boundary alongside the function — what we cover, and where the responsibility passes to the hospital, the patient or a third party.

下方每项服务都同时写明"我们做什么"与"边界在哪里" — 我们覆盖的范围，以及责任在哪里转移到医院、患者本人或第三方。

01

Bilingual Communication

双语沟通

English and Chinese as default; French, Japanese and Russian available by reservation; other languages require a third-party medical interpreter booked at least 5 working days in advance.

中英双语为默认；法、日、俄通过预约支持；其他语种 5 工作日前预约第三方医学翻译。

Boundary. Covers clinical and administrative scenarios only; not for tourism, business negotiation or other settings.

边界。仅覆盖临床场景与行政沟通，不含旅游观光、商务谈判等场景翻译。

02

Appointment Scheduling

预约协调

Schedule alignment with senior specialists at BFH·IMD and partner hospitals; a coordination plan is typically delivered within 2–5 working days.

与 BFH·IMD 及合作医院的资深专家档期对接；通常 2–5 个工作日内出方案。

Boundary. Slots are subject to the physician's actual schedule; we cannot guarantee a date you specify.

边界。档期受医生实际工作安排约束；不保证您指定的具体日期。

03

In-Hospital Support

院内陪同

On the day of screening, examination or consultation, your coordinator is present on-site throughout.

体检日 / 检查日 / 会诊日，协调员到现场陪诊全程。

Boundary. We accompany you to the consultation-room door. Our role is to help you navigate the healthcare system; medical decisions remain between you and your physician.

边界。我们陪您到诊室门口。我们的职责是帮您在医疗体系中找到正确路径；医疗决策由您和医师共同做出。

04

Document Preparation

资料准备

A personalised preparation checklist is sent 5–7 days before arrival, covering fasting, medication holds, required documents and how to organise prior records.

行前 5–7 天发送专属准备清单（含禁食 / 停药 / 必带文件 / 病历整理建议）。

Boundary. The checklist is based on the hospital's general requirements and your specific scenario; the hospital's same-day instructions take final precedence.

边界。准备清单基于医院通用要求 + 您的具体场景；最终以医院当日实际要求为准。

05

Report Delivery & Interpretation Support

报告解释支持

Coordination of English summary translation and scheduling of remote or on-site report discussion with BFH physicians.

协调英文摘要翻译 + 协调与 BFH 医师的远程或现场解读会档期。

Boundary. YuanU does not interpret reports. Clinical interpretation is performed by BFH physicians.

边界。远游不解读报告；解读由 BFH 医师完成。

06

Privacy Protection

隐私保护

Encrypted email and file transfer; report transfer requires your written authorisation; internal access to your records is restricted; working copies are destroyed in accordance with YuanU's internal retention policy.

加密邮件与文件传输 / 报告传递需您书面授权 / 远游内部限制接触权限 / 工作副本按远游内部留存政策销毁。

Boundary. Compliant with the PRC Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) and hospital privacy requirements; YuanU does not share your medical information with any third party.

边界。遵循中国《个人信息保护法》及医院隐私要求；不向任何第三方分享您的医疗信息。

07

Post-Visit Coordination

后续协调

One round of remote follow-up after you return home is included as standard; additional rounds are charged by the hour.

回国后 1 轮远程随访（含在标准服务）；超出部分按小时计。

Boundary. This does not replace your home-country physician's follow-up and does not constitute remote medical guidance.

边界。不替代您本国医生的随访；不做远程医学指导。

08

Specialty Referral

专科转介

If an examination reveals abnormalities or deeper consultation is needed, we assist in connecting you to other specialties at BFH·IMD or partner hospitals.

如检查异常或需要更深入的会诊，协助衔接 BFH·IMD 其他专科或合作医院。

Boundary. The referral itself carries no additional fee; coordination on the new service line is charged under that line's rules.

边界。转介本身不收取额外费用；转介后协调费按新条线规则核算。

Coordinator role · client view 协调员角色 · 客户视角

What your YuanU Patient Coordinator actually does for you

作为客户，远游协调员怎么帮您

What you purchase from YuanU is not "medical services" — it is accessibility of medical services plus end-to-end coordination. A YuanU Patient Coordinator (bilingual, non-clinical liaison) plays the following roles throughout your visit:

您从远游购买的不是"医疗服务"，而是"医疗服务的可达性 + 全流程协调"。一名 YuanU Patient Coordinator（双语、非临床联络人）会在您的整个就医过程中扮演以下角色：

  • Pre-trip project manager. Assembles your travel dates, physician availability, visa window, pre-arrival paperwork, hotel and transport into an executable timeline. 行前的项目经理。帮您把出行日期、医生档期、签证窗口、行前材料、酒店与接送排成一张可执行的时间表。
  • On-arrival local contact. Meets you at the airport or hotel, familiarises you with BFH·IMD's layout, and delivers you from hotel doorstep to consultation-room door. 抵达后的当地联络人。接机或到酒店、熟悉 BFH·IMD 路径、把您从酒店门口顺利交付到诊室门口。
  • Bilingual interpreter at the consultation door. Accompanies you into the consultation, translates your questions accurately to the physician and the physician's answers accurately to you — without addition, omission or interpretation. 诊室门口的双语翻译。陪您进入诊室、把您的问题准确翻译给医生、把医生的话准确翻译给您（不增减、不解读）。
  • Administrative responder during examinations. Handles on-the-spot needs for adding items, signatures, rescheduling and payments. 检查中的行政响应。当现场出现需要追加项目、签字、改期、付款等环节时，协调员第一时间响应。
  • Report-delivery and home-country handover. Once a report is ready, arranges English summary translation, encrypted delivery to you or your home-country physician, and one round of remote follow-up. 报告交付与回国后衔接。报告就绪后翻译英文摘要 → 加密传递给您或您本国医师 → 1 轮远程随访。

What the coordinator does NOT do

协调员不做的事

  • Make medical decisions on your behalf with the physician. 不替您与医生做医学决策。
  • Interpret your medical reports. 不解读您的报告。
  • Issue any medical recommendation. 不开任何医学建议。
  • Answer clinical questions in the physician's absence. 不在医师不在场的情况下回答临床问题。

Your Patient Journey — six stages

您的就医旅程 — 6 个阶段

从您的第一封咨询到回国后随访共 6 个阶段。每个阶段以三栏呈现：远游做什么、您做什么、时间节点。

Stage 01 · Before you arrive 阶段 01 · 抵达北京之前

Inquiry & Medical Records Review

咨询与病历审核

What YuanU does

远游做什么

A YuanU Patient Coordinator provides the first reply within one Beijing working day (CST 9:00–18:00, Mon–Fri). The coordinator requests records needed for an eligibility review and confirms your preferred language and time zone.

北京工作时间内（CST 9:00–18:00 周一至周五）1 个工作日内由一名 YuanU Patient Coordinator 首次回复；请您提供初步病历，确认您的偏好语言与时区。

What you do

您做什么

Submit the inquiry via form or email. Begin gathering: records from the past 3 months, prior examination reports / imaging (DICOM preferred), current medication list, allergy history, prior endoscopy / pathology reports if any.

通过表单或邮件提交咨询；开始整理：近 3 个月病历、既往检查报告 / 影像（DICOM 优先）、当前用药清单、过敏史、既往内镜 / 病理报告（如有）。

Timeline

时间节点

First reply within 1 working day. Full records review and clarifying questions across 1–3 working days.

首次回复 1 个工作日内；完整病历审核与追问 1–3 个工作日。

1–3 working days 1–3 个工作日
Stage 02 · Before you arrive 阶段 02 · 抵达北京之前

Eligibility Confirmation, Coordination Plan & Visa Support Letter

接诊条件确认 + 协调方案 + 签证支持函

What YuanU does

远游做什么

Confirms eligibility with BFH·IMD; proposes a plan: recommended package / physician / date options, plus an estimate of YuanU's coordination fee and the hospital's estimated medical cost. Once you confirm, issues an appointment confirmation letter within 1–2 working days for visa application support.

与 BFH·IMD 沟通后，给出"建议套餐 / 建议医生 / 可选日期 / 远游服务费 + 医院端预估费用"；您确认后，1–2 个工作日内出具预约确认函，可用于支持您的中国医疗签证申请。

What you do

您做什么

Review the proposed plan, choose a date and confirm. Begin your visa application at your local Chinese embassy or consulate using the appointment confirmation letter as a supporting document.

审阅协调方案，选定日期并确认；持预约确认函作为支持材料，在您所在国的中国驻外使领馆办理签证申请。

Timeline

时间节点

Coordination plan within 2–5 working days (subject to physician schedule). Confirmation letter within 1–2 working days after your confirmation.

协调方案 2–5 个工作日（受医生档期影响）；您确认后 1–2 个工作日出函。

2–5 working days 2–5 个工作日

Clear boundary. Visa applications are processed by the Chinese embassies and consulates in your country. YuanU cannot issue a visa invitation letter, cannot file your visa application on your behalf, and cannot influence the visa decision.

明确边界。签证申请由您在所在国的中国驻外使领馆办理；远游不能签发邀请函、不能代办签证、不能影响签证审批结果。

Stage 03 · Before you arrive 阶段 03 · 抵达北京之前

Pre-arrival Preparation

行前准备

What YuanU does

远游做什么

5–7 days before departure, your coordinator sends a personalised checklist: fasting / medication-hold instructions, required documents, hotel and transfer confirmations, local connectivity and payment guidance, and the day-by-day schedule.

出发前 5–7 天，协调员发送专属准备清单：禁食 / 停药要求、必带文件清单、酒店与接送确认、当地通讯与支付建议、当日时间表。

What you do

您做什么

Follow the fasting and medication-hold guidance; gather the required documents (passport, prior records, current prescriptions); confirm flight and hotel details; reach out with any last-minute questions.

按禁食 / 停药要求执行；准备好必带文件（护照、既往病历、当前处方）；确认航班与酒店；如有最后疑问随时联系协调员。

Timeline

时间节点

Checklist delivered 5–7 days before departure; coordinator stays reachable through your departure.

出发前 5–7 天发送清单；至您出发前协调员持续可联系。

5–7 days before departure 出发前 5–7 天

Arrival & In-hospital Coordination at BFH·IMD

检查、会诊与现场双语支持

在协调员翻译下与医师就临床问题沟通；提出您希望被翻译的额外问题；签署医院要求的同意书等文件。

1–3 days, depending on package complexity and consultation needs.

1–3 天（视套餐复杂度与会诊需要）。

1–3 days 1–3 天

明确边界。所有医学操作由 BFH·IMD 医师独立完成；协调员不参与任何医学判断。

Stage 06 · After you return home 阶段 06 · 回国之后

Report Delivery, English Summary & One Round of Remote Follow-up

报告交付 + 英文摘要 + 1 轮随访

What YuanU does

远游做什么

Same-day reports: ready the same or next day. Pathology reports: 5–7 working days. English summary: delivered within 2–3 working days after each report is ready via encrypted channel to you or your home-country physician. Within one month of your return, coordinates one round of remote follow-up (included in the standard service).

当日报告：当日或次日出；病理报告：5–7 个工作日；英文摘要：报告就绪后 2–3 个工作日内通过加密渠道发送给您 / 您本国医师；回国后 1 个月内远游协调员安排 1 轮远程随访（含在标准协调服务中）。

What you do

您做什么

Authorise the recipient of report transfer; review the English summary; prepare questions for the follow-up call (and forward them to your home-country physician if needed).

授权报告接收方；审阅英文摘要；为随访沟通准备问题（如有本国医师可同步转发）。

Timeline

时间节点

1–4 weeks after the examination day, depending on pathology turnaround. Additional rounds of follow-up beyond the included one are charged by the hour.

检查后 1–4 周（视病理出具时间）；超出 1 轮的额外随访按小时计。

1–4 weeks after exam 检查后 1–4 周

Service Boundary. At every stage above, all medical actions — diagnosis, examination, prescription, treatment and report interpretation — are performed by licensed physicians at BFH·IMD and partner hospitals. YuanU Patient Coordinators handle administration, interpretation and handover only, and do not participate in any clinical judgement. The full boundary statement is on the About page.

服务边界。以上每一步中所有医学动作（诊断、检查、用药、治疗、报告解读）的主体均为 BFH·IMD 及合作医院的执业医师。远游患者协调员负责行政、翻译与衔接，不参与任何医学判断。完整服务边界见 About 页。
