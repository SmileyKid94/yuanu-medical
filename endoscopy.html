§ 5.2 · Why endoscopy § 5.2 · 为何做胃肠镜

1. Why these screenings matter

1. 胃肠镜筛查的意义

YuanU does not generate or estimate epidemiological data; the descriptions below are summarized from publicly available sources (WHO / IARC / China National Cancer Center).

远游不自行估算流行病学数据；以下描述综合自公开来源（WHO / IARC / 国家癌症中心）。

In East Asian populations, gastric cancer and colorectal cancer have long been among the most common malignancies. This is one of the main reasons international guidelines generally recommend routine screening for at-risk age groups.

在东亚人群中，胃癌与结直肠癌长期位列高发恶性肿瘤之列；这是国际指南普遍建议适龄人群规律筛查的主要原因之一。

Both upper-GI cancer (including gastric cancer) and colorectal cancer share the following features:

上消化道癌（含胃癌）与结直肠癌均具备以下特征：

  • Early symptoms are non-specific: most patients are already at an intermediate or advanced stage by the time obvious symptoms appear.
  • 早期症状不典型：多数患者在出现明显症状时已属中晚期。
  • Early lesions are actionable: polyps, low-grade intraepithelial neoplasia, and early cancers detected early can often be removed or treated endoscopically, with markedly different treatment complexity and prognosis.
  • 早期可干预：早期发现的息肉、低级别上皮内瘤变、早癌，多可在内镜下完成切除或局部治疗，治疗复杂度与预后差异显著。
  • Established screening tools: gastroscopy and colonoscopy are recognized as the most direct and complete screening methods for these cancers.
  • 筛查工具成熟：胃镜与肠镜是目前公认对应癌种最直接、最完整的筛查方式。

Who this is most relevant for

尤其适用于以下国际患者

International patients from regions with a high incidence of gastric / colorectal cancer, those with a family history, or those with long-term high-risk lifestyle factors (smoking, alcohol, long-term high-salt / red-meat diet) may find it clinically reasonable to undertake systematic endoscopic screening at one of mainland China's most resource-dense tertiary hospitals.

对于来自胃癌 / 结直肠癌高发地区、有家族史、或长期处于高危生活方式（吸烟、饮酒、长期高盐 / 红肉饮食）的国际患者，在中国大陆密度最高的三甲资源中完成系统性内镜筛查，是一个临床合理的选择。

§ 5.3 · Who it's for § 5.3 · 适合谁

2. Who may need it

2. 适合人群

Whether to undergo screening, what type, and at what interval should be decided by the attending physician at intake.

最终是否需要做、做哪种、间隔多久，请以接诊医师建议为准。

The following groups are commonly advised to consider systematic gastric / colonic endoscopic screening:

以下人群通常被建议考虑系统性胃 / 肠内镜筛查：

  • Adults aged 40 or above undergoing their first systematic check-up
  • 40 岁及以上首次系统体检者
  • A first-degree family history of gastric or colorectal cancer
  • 有胃癌 / 结直肠癌一级亲属家族史
  • Long-term upper-GI symptoms (recurrent abdominal pain, acid reflux, dyspepsia, dysphagia)
  • 长期上消化道症状（反复腹痛、反酸、消化不良、吞咽困难）
  • Long-term lower-GI symptoms (changes in bowel habits, blood in stool, unexplained constipation / diarrhea)
  • 长期下消化道症状（排便习惯改变、便血、不明原因便秘 / 腹泻）
  • Prior H. pylori (gastric helicobacter) positive, treated but not re-tested
  • 既往 H. pylori（幽门螺杆菌）阳性、已治疗未复查者
  • Long-term smoking / drinking; long-term high-salt, red-meat, or highly-processed-meat diet
  • 长期吸烟 / 饮酒、长期高盐 / 红肉 / 高加工肉饮食人群
  • Prior endoscopy showing polyps / atrophic gastritis / intestinal metaplasia / intraepithelial neoplasia, requiring follow-up per physician advice
  • 既往内镜检查发现息肉 / 萎缩性胃炎 / 肠化生 / 上皮内瘤变，需按医生建议复查者

Reminder: the list above is for reference only and does not constitute medical advice. Whether to undergo screening, what type, and at what interval is determined by the attending physician at BFH·IMD after intake.

提示：以上清单仅供参考，不构成医学建议。是否做、做哪种、间隔多久，由 BFH·IMD 接诊医师面诊后判断。

Gastroscopy (EGD — Esophagogastroduodenoscopy)胃镜 / Gastroscopy (EGD) Dimension维度 Colonoscopy肠镜 / Colonoscopy

§ 5.8 · Abnormal findings § 5.8 · 异常结果处理

7. If polyps or abnormal findings are found

7. 如果发现息肉或异常

How BFH·IMD handles abnormal findings — and what YuanU does (and does not do) at this stage.

BFH·IMD 如何处理异常结果 — 以及远游在这一阶段做什么、不做什么。

BFH·IMD physician's clinical handling

BFH·IMD 内镜医师的临床处理

  • Small polyps (< 1 cm, regular shape): typically removed endoscopically (polypectomy / EMR) during the same exam, with specimens sent for pathology.
  • 小息肉（< 1 cm，形态规整）：通常在同次检查中直接行内镜下切除（息肉切除术 / EMR），并送病理。
  • Larger polyps / early suspicious lesions: may require a separately scheduled EMR / ESD (endoscopic mucosal / submucosal resection).
  • 较大息肉 / 早期可疑病变：可能需要单独安排 EMR / ESD（内镜下黏膜切除 / 剥离术）。
  • Suspicious malignant / invasive lesions: the physician will recommend further imaging, multidisciplinary (MDT) consultation, biopsy review, and similar work-up.
  • 可疑恶性 / 浸润性病变：医师会建议进一步影像评估、多学科会诊（MDT）、活检结果会诊等。

What YuanU coordinators do in this scenario

远游协调员在异常结果中的工作

  1. Coordinate a 30–45 min results-discussion meeting with the BFH·IMD attending physician (billed by the hospital under its specialist consultation fee).
  2. 协助您与 BFH·IMD 主诊医师安排 30–45 分钟的结果解读会（医院按其专家会诊费用单独计费）。
  3. Coordinate the next-step exam or MDT appointment.
  4. 协调下一步检查 / 多学科会诊的预约。
  5. Translate and explain the hospital's next-step recommendation (translation, not medical interpretation).
  6. 翻译并解释医院给出的下一步建议（翻译，不是医学解读）。
  7. Assist with transmitting the report and imaging through an encrypted channel to your home-country physician.
  8. 协助报告与影像通过加密渠道传递给您本国医师。

Explicit limit: YuanU coordinators do not interpret pathology reports, do not judge malignancy, and do not recommend treatment plans. All medical judgments are made independently by licensed physicians at BFH·IMD and partner hospitals.

明确边界：远游协调员不解读病理报告、不判断恶性程度、不建议治疗方案。所有医学判断由 BFH·IMD 及合作医院的执业医师独立完成。
