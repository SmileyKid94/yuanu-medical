About YuanU关于远游

About YuanU — A Licensed Medical Coordination Service Company in Beijing

关于远游 — 一家持照运营的医疗协调服务公司

YuanU exists for one specific reason: international patients who need Beijing-grade specialist care should not have to negotiate with the Chinese hospital system in Chinese. We do not provide medical care — we make Beijing medicine reachable.

远游存在的理由很具体：需要北京级专家诊疗的海外患者，不应该被迫用中文和中国医院系统打交道。我们不做诊疗——我们让北京医疗对海外患者"够得着"。

Beijing YuanU International Travel Co., Ltd. was founded in 2006 and holds a travel-agency operating license (No. L-BJ-CJ00262) issued by the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture and Tourism. Since establishing our inbound medical coordination line, YuanU has operated under the brand YuanU Medical Concierge, focused on coordinating access for international patients to senior medical resources in Beijing.

北京远游国际旅行社有限责任公司（Beijing YuanU International Travel Co., Ltd.）成立于 2006 年，依法持有北京市文化和旅游局颁发的旅行社业务经营许可证（L-BJ-CJ00262）。自入境医疗业务条线建立以来，远游以 YuanU Medical Concierge 品牌运营，专注于为海外患者提供北京顶级医疗资源的协调与对接服务。

Company history公司沿革

From a travel agency to medical coordination — why this foundation fits

从旅行社到入境医疗协调 — 为什么这个底盘合适

YuanU was founded in 2006 as an international travel agency, and for many years specialized in inbound visitor coordination for clients from Asia, the Middle East and the CIS.

The travel-agency license covers exactly the capabilities that international medical patients need beyond clinical care — visa-support documentation, cross-border payment and invoicing compliance, foreign-language reception and a working interpreter team, airport transfer, and administrative response in unexpected situations. These are not incidental skills; they are what the past two decades of inbound-coordination work has built up at YuanU.

Building on that foundation, YuanU established a long-term coordination partnership with Beijing Friendship Hospital's International Medical Department (BFH·IMD) and shifted its primary focus toward medical coordination. Today, YuanU Medical Concierge operates as our dedicated medical-coordination brand, covering three core service lines: health screening, gastroscopy and colonoscopy, and senior-attending-level specialist consultations.

远游成立于 2006 年，最初以国际旅行社业务起家，长期承接面向亚洲、中东、独联体等地区的入境接待与跨境后勤项目。

旅行社许可所覆盖的资质 — 入境接待资质、行程文件出具能力、跨境支付与开票合规、外宾接待与翻译团队 — 在医疗协调场景中天然适配：海外患者来京就医需要的不只是“约到医生”，还需要签证支持函、抵京接送、医院附近住宿、本地通讯与支付配置、紧急情况下的行政响应。这些都是远游过去近 20 年累积的能力底盘。

自入境医疗业务条线建立以来，远游与北京友谊医院国际医疗部（BFH·IMD）建立长期协作关系，逐步把业务重心从“旅行接待”转向“医疗协调”。今天，YuanU Medical Concierge 作为远游对外的医疗协调服务品牌运营，覆盖健康体检、胃肠镜检查、主任医师级专家会诊三个核心条线。

What we coordinate

我们做协调

We connect international patients' care intentions with the available schedules of senior-attending-level specialists at BFH·IMD and partnering Beijing hospitals.

把海外患者的就医意向，准确对接到 BFH·IMD 及合作医院的资深专家档期上。

How we coordinate

我们做对接

Every international patient is assigned a single YuanU Patient Coordinator (bilingual · non-clinical liaison) who works with you from the first email — covering pre-visit document preparation, in-room accompaniment, bilingual interpretation in clinical settings, report delivery, and one round of remote follow-up after return.

每一位国际患者由一名 YuanU Patient Coordinator（远游患者协调员，双语、非临床联络人）从第一封邮件起全程负责，覆盖行前材料准备、院内陪诊、临床场景双语翻译、报告交付、回国后远程随访。

What we don't do

我们不做的事

YuanU is not a medical institution. We do not diagnose, do not prescribe, do not issue medical opinions, and do not make any promises about clinical outcomes. All clinical judgments are made independently by the attending physicians at BFH·IMD and partnering hospitals.

远游不是医疗机构，不诊断、不开方、不出具医学意见、不对医疗结果做承诺；所有临床判断由 BFH 及合作医院的执业医师独立完成。

Working relationship with Beijing medical resources与北京医疗资源的协作关系

Honest about the relationship — a long-term partnership, not a subsidiary

诚实地说明关系 — 长期协作，而非附属

Core partner: Beijing Friendship Hospital · International Medical Department (BFH·IMD)

核心协作机构：北京友谊医院国际医疗部（BFH·IMD）

Beijing Friendship Hospital is a Class A tertiary general hospital directly under the National Health Commission and a principal teaching hospital of Capital Medical University. The International Medical Department is an independently established clinical unit within the hospital, providing multilingual reception (English, French, Japanese, Russian) with its own clinic area, examination pathway and multidisciplinary collaboration mechanism.

北京友谊医院是国家卫健委直属的三级甲等综合医院、首都医科大学附属教学医院。国际医疗部（International Medical Department）是医院内独立设置的科室，提供英、法、日、俄等多语言接待，配备独立的诊区、检查路径与多学科协作机制。

YuanU has a long-term working relationship with BFH·IMD, with stable working procedures established for appointment coordination, in-room accompaniment pathways, report delivery, and follow-on referrals for abnormal findings.

远游与 BFH·IMD 建立长期协作关系：在预约协调、院内陪诊路径、报告交付、异常结果衔接等环节已形成稳定的工作机制。

To be clear: YuanU is not a subsidiary or exclusive agent of BFH·IMD. BFH·IMD is an independent department of a public hospital and is open to any patient who meets its admission criteria. What YuanU adds is cross-border coordination, bilingual liaison, and end-to-end process management.

需要说明的是：远游不是 BFH·IMD 的下属机构或独家代理；BFH·IMD 是公立医院的独立科室，对所有符合接诊条件的患者开放。远游提供的价值在于跨境对接、双语协调与全流程管理。

What YuanU Does — and What We Don't

远游服务边界 — 我们做什么、不做什么

YuanU Medical Concierge (Beijing YuanU International Travel Co., Ltd.) is a licensed medical coordination service company. We are not a medical institution. We do not diagnose, prescribe, perform procedures, or guarantee medical outcomes. All clinical decisions and treatments are provided exclusively by the licensed physicians at Beijing Friendship Hospital's International Medical Department (BFH·IMD) and other partnering hospitals.

远游医疗（北京远游国际旅行社有限责任公司）是一家依法注册的医疗协调服务公司，不是医疗机构。我们不提供诊断、不开具处方、不实施医疗操作，也不就医疗结果做任何承诺。所有临床判断与治疗均由北京友谊医院·国际医疗部（BFH·IMD）及合作医院的执业医师独立提供。

What we do

我们做什么

  • Based on the medical records you provide, coordinate consultations and examination appointments with senior-attending-level specialists at BFH·IMD;
  • Full-process bilingual coordination: pre-visit document preparation, in-room accompaniment, medical interpretation in clinical settings, and post-examination follow-up;
  • Issue an appointment confirmation letter that may be used to support your Chinese medical-visa application; whether the visa is ultimately granted is decided independently by the Chinese embassy or consulate in your home country;
  • As needed, arrange airport transfer, recommendations for hotels within walking distance of the hospital, and configuration of local communications and payment tools.
  • 基于您提供的病历信息，协调您与 BFH·IMD 主任医师级专家的会诊及检查预约；
  • 全程双语协调：行前材料准备、院内陪诊、临床场景医学翻译、检查后续随访；
  • 出具用于中国医疗签证申请的预约确认函；但签证最终是否签发，由您所在国的中国驻外使领馆独立决定；
  • 根据需要，安排接送机、医院步行可达酒店推荐、本地通讯与支付工具配置等行程支持。

What we don't do

我们不做什么

  • We do not provide medical advice, diagnostic opinions or second opinions; any medical question must be addressed directly to your attending physician at BFH·IMD;
  • We do not make commitments about any specific examination result, biopsy conclusion or treatment plan; clinical conclusions are issued independently by the physician after the examination has been performed;
  • We cannot issue a visa invitation letter, cannot apply for a visa on your behalf, and cannot influence visa-approval decisions;
  • We do not advance hospital medical fees on your behalf; medical fees are paid by you directly to the hospital at the time of service. YuanU's coordination service fee is quoted to you before travel as a separate, transparent line item;
  • We do not provide emergency or after-hours clinical services; if a medical emergency arises during your stay in Beijing, please call 120 (Beijing emergency services) immediately — YuanU will then provide administrative support at the earliest opportunity.
  • 不提供医疗建议、诊断意见或第二诊疗意见；任何医疗问题须直接向 BFH·IMD 主诊医师咨询；
  • 不对任何具体检查结果、活检结论或治疗方案做承诺；医疗结论由临床医生在为您实施检查后独立给出；
  • 不能签发签证邀请函、不能代办签证、不能影响签证审批结果；
  • 不代垫医院医疗费；医疗费由您在就诊时直接支付给医院。远游的协调服务费在您行前作为独立、透明的费用单独报价；
  • 不提供急诊或非工作时间临床服务；如您在京期间发生医疗急症，请立即拨打 120（北京急救），我们会在第一时间提供行政支持。

When a request is beyond our scope

超出能力范围时

If your needs fall outside what BFH·IMD or our coordination model can responsibly cover — for example, organ transplantation, emergency interventions, or treatment in specialties outside the scope of our partnering physicians — we will say so plainly and, where possible, recommend a more suitable path, even if that means referring you to another service provider.

如果您的需求超出 BFH·IMD 或我们协调模式可以负责任覆盖的范围 — 如器官移植、急诊干预、合作专家学科范围之外的治疗 — 我们会明确告知，并在条件允许的情况下推荐更合适的路径，即便这意味着将您转介给其他服务提供方。
