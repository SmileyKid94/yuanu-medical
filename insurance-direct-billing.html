A. Executive Summary

A. 概要

BFH-IMD holds direct-billing (cashless) agreements with 23 commercial insurance/TPA partners as physically displayed on its Shunyi campus signage board titled "保险直付合作 / INSURANCE DIRECT PAYMENT COOPERATION" as of 2025-11 (新浪财经 2025-11-24). The identical count of 23 was independently confirmed at BFH's Third Commercial Insurance Salon on 2025-01-27, where "23家商业保险领域的重要公司代表参会" (第三届商保沙龙 2025-01-27).

截至 2025 年 11 月，BFH-IMD 与 23 家商业保险/TPA 合作伙伴签有直付（免现金）协议，这一数据来自其顺义院区标牌“保险直付合作 / INSURANCE DIRECT PAYMENT COOPERATION”（新浪财经 2025-11-24）。2025 年 1 月 27 日，在 BFH 第三届商保沙龙上，独立确认了相同的 23 家数字，“23家商业保险领域的重要公司代表参会”（第三届商保沙龙 2025-01-27）。

Approximately 10 of those 23 partners are TPAs or TPA-hybrid entities (MediLink-Global, MSH China, CHC 商保通, Prosper 柏盛健康, KRAY Assistance, Windstone 风石健康, Blue Pass 镁信, D Health, 致保健康, FESCO). Each TPA intermediates direct-billing access for multiple upstream international insurers. Through these TPA relationships, effective direct-billing coverage at BFH-IMD extends to 30+ international insurers — exceeding the headline 23 count.

在这 23 家合作伙伴中，约 10 家为 TPA 或 TPA 混合实体（中间带 MediLink-Global、MSH 万欣和、商保通 CHC、柏盛健康 Prosper、康瑞救援 KRAY Assistance、风石健康 Windstone、镁信 Blue Pass、D Health、致保健康、FESCO）。每家 TPA 为其多个上游国际保险公司提供直付通道。通过这些 TPA 关系，BFH-IMD 的实际直付覆盖范围扩展至 30+ 家国际保险公司——超过标称的 23 家。

4 entities are explicitly excluded: WellAway (China excluded from ORBE Gold Zone 2 coverage), Pacific Prime (broker only), Bellwood Prestbury (B2B broker only), and InterGlobal (historical brand merged into AXA). Vanbreda International is also historical (merged into Cigna).
