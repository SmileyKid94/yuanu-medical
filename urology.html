§ 1 · Overview § 1 · 概述

1. Common urology conditions

1. 泌尿外科常见就诊场景

YuanU coordinates care across a range of common urology visit scenarios. The following list describes typical situations — it is not exhaustive and does not constitute medical advice.

远游协调覆盖多种常见泌尿外科就诊场景。以下列举典型情况，非完整清单，亦不构成医学建议。

  • Kidney stones (renal calculi) — coordinating evaluation, imaging follow-up, and specialist consultation for stone management.
  • 肾结石 — 协调结石相关评估、影像随访及专科问诊。
  • Benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) — coordinating consultations for lower urinary tract symptoms and prostate-related concerns.
  • 前列腺增生（BPH） — 协调下尿路症状及前列腺相关问题的就诊咨询。
  • Urological tumors — coordinating evaluation and specialist referral for tumors of the kidney, bladder, or prostate.
  • 泌尿系肿瘤 — 协调肾、膀胱、前列腺等部位肿瘤的评估与专科转诊。
  • Urinary tract infections (UTI) — coordinating visits for recurrent or complicated urinary infections.
  • 尿路感染 — 协调反复发作或复杂性尿路感染的就诊。
  • Laparoscopic urological surgery — coordinating pre-operative evaluation, surgical scheduling, and post-operative follow-up for minimally invasive procedures.
  • 腹腔镜泌尿外科手术 — 协调微创手术的术前评估、手术排期及术后随访。

Important note

重要提示

YuanU is a coordination service. We do not provide medical opinions, recommend specific examinations, or suggest clinical pathways. All decisions about diagnosis and treatment are made by the licensed attending physician at the partner hospital.

远游是协调服务机构。我们不提供医学意见、不建议具体检查项目、不规划临床路径。所有诊断与治疗决策均由合作医院执业医师做出。

§ 2 · Why Beijing § 2 · 为何选择北京

2. Why Beijing for urology care

2. 北京泌尿外科就医优势

Beijing is home to several of China's leading urology departments, offering high outpatient volume and deep expertise in both open and minimally invasive urological surgery. Key advantages for international patients include:

北京拥有多个全国领先的泌尿外科科室，门诊量大，在开放手术与微创泌尿外科手术领域均有深厚积累。对国际患者的主要优势包括：

  • PUMCH (Peking Union Medical College Hospital): its urology department is widely recognized as one of the top programs in China, with broad expertise in complex urological conditions.
  • PUMCH（北京协和医院）：泌尿外科是全国公认的领先学科之一，在复杂泌尿疾病方面具有广泛经验。
  • Peking University First Hospital (北大医院): its Institute of Urology is one of the earliest and most established urology centers in China, known for research and clinical breadth.
  • 北京大学第一医院（北大医院）：其泌尿外科研究所是中国最早建立的泌尿外科中心之一，以科研与临床覆盖面见长。
  • Beijing Friendship Hospital (BFH): urology department with experience in minimally invasive surgical techniques for common urological conditions.
  • 北京友谊医院（BFH）：泌尿外科在常见泌尿疾病的微创手术技术方面经验丰富。
  • Beijing Chaoyang Hospital: urology department known for endourology and laparoscopic surgical experience.
  • 北京朝阳医院：泌尿外科以腔内泌尿外科与腹腔镜手术经验著称。

Hospitals we coordinate with

我们协调合作的医院

YuanU coordinates care at multiple tertiary hospitals in Beijing, including PUMCH, BFH-IMD (Beijing Friendship Hospital International Medical Department), and others depending on the patient's needs and physician availability.

远游在北京多家三甲医院提供协调服务，包括 PUMCH（北京协和医院）、BFH-IMD（北京友谊医院国际医疗部）等，具体视患者需求与医师排班而定。

Hospital choice is based on clinical fit and availability, not on any commercial arrangement. YuanU does not receive referral fees from any hospital.

医院选择基于临床匹配度与可预约性，不涉及任何商业安排。远游不收取任何医院的转介费用。

§ 6 · Service Boundary § 6 · 服务边界

6. Service Boundary & disclaimer

6. 服务边界与免责

YuanU is a coordination service, not a medical institution

远游是协调服务机构，非医疗机构

YuanU provides administrative coordination and bilingual interpretation for urology visits at partner tertiary hospitals. We do not provide medical diagnoses, do not recommend specific examinations or treatment plans, and do not interpret clinical results. All medical decisions are made independently by licensed physicians at the partner hospitals.

远游为合作三甲医院的泌尿外科就诊提供行政协调与双语翻译服务。我们不提供医学诊断、不建议具体检查或治疗方案、不解读临床结果。所有医学决策均由合作医院的执业医师独立做出。
